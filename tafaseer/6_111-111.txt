Those who reject belief demanded that Allah and the angels be shown to them face-to-face, claiming they would not believe unless they received what the messengers received. They also asked why the angels were not sent to them or why they could not see their Lord. Allah responded by revealing that even if all nations were gathered before them, one after another, to testify to the truth of the messengers' messages, they would still not believe unless Allah willed. Guidance belongs to Allah alone, and He does as He wills. He cannot be questioned about His actions, while others will be questioned. Allah also made it clear that those against whom His wrath has been decreed will not believe, even if every sign were shown to them, until they witness the painful torment.
