Allah's justice is affirmed, as He does not change the bounty granted to a people unless they commit evil. This principle is illustrated through the example of the people of Pharaoh, who were punished by Allah for denying His signs. Allah took away the blessings they once enjoyed, such as gardens, springs, and treasures, in retribution for their sins. They wronged themselves, not Allah, who had granted them these favors. The punishment came as a result of their own actions, and Allah’s decree is irreversible when He wills it.
