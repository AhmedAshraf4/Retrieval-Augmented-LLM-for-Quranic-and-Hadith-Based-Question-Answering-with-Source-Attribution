Tafsir Explanation: A delegation sent by the King of Ethiopia, An-Najashi, came to meet the Prophet Muhammad and hear the Qur'an. Upon hearing it, they embraced Islam and returned to An-Najashi, sharing their experience. These individuals, followers of the religion of Jesus, were moved by the Qur'an and became Muslims. Allah reveals that the Jews are the strongest in enmity toward the believers, as they opposed the truth, belittled others, and even sought to kill the Prophet. In contrast, the Christians are described as being closer in love to the believers, as their faith is rooted in mercy and kindness, influenced by the teachings of Jesus. Many of them humbly accepted the Qur'an as the truth, wishing to join the righteous in Paradise. In reward, Allah promises them eternal gardens beneath which rivers flow, while those who disbelieve and oppose Allah’s signs are destined for the Hellfire.
