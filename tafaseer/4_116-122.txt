Tafsīr Explanation: Shirk, or associating partners with Allah, is unforgivable. Idolaters, by worshiping beings like angels as deities, stray far from the true path and invite destruction in both this life and the Hereafter. They are, in essence, worshiping Shaytan, who leads them astray by making their actions seem appealing. Shaytan deceives them with false promises, such as convincing them to change Allah’s creation through acts like tattooing or mutilating animals. Those who follow him suffer irreparable loss, as they choose Shaytan over Allah. On the Day of Judgment, Shaytan will admit his betrayal, and those who believed his lies will face eternal punishment in Hell, with no escape. In contrast, those who believe in Allah and perform righteous deeds will be rewarded with eternal Paradise, where rivers flow beneath their gardens, fulfilling Allah’s true promise, as He is the most truthful.
