Idolators are not fit to maintain the Masjids of Allah, including Al-Masjid Al-Haram, the holiest mosque built by Prophet Ibrahim for the exclusive worship of Allah. Idolators testify to their disbelief through their actions and statements, as they follow false beliefs like Christianity, Judaism, or polytheism. Allah declares their deeds null and void due to their shirk and condemns them to eternal punishment in the Fire. True maintainers of the Masjids are those who believe in Allah and the Last Day, perform the obligatory prayers, give zakah, and fear only Allah. These believers are on the true path to guidance, and their actions are the hallmark of their faith, as those who maintain the Masjids fulfill the promises made by Allah. Their reward is the ultimate success and the possibility of being raised to a station of honor on the Day of Resurrection, as Allah will grant the Prophet Muhammad intercession at Maqam Mahmud.
