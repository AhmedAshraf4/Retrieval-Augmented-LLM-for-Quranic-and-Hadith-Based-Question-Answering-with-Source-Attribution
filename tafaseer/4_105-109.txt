Tafsir Explanation: Allah commands His Messenger Muhammad to judge between people based on what He has revealed, affirming the truth of His Book. In a narration, the Prophet explained that he judges based on what he hears, acknowledging that some may present their cases more persuasively than others. He warned that if he judged in favor of someone regarding the rights of another, it would be a piece of the Fire, which would be tied around the unjust person's neck on the Day of Resurrection. He advised two men with a dispute over inheritance, lacking evidence, to divide the inheritance justly, drawing lots, and forgiving each other afterward. Allah then reprimands the hypocrites who hide their wrongdoings from people but cannot conceal them from Allah, who knows their hearts and actions. These hypocrites, despite winning judgments in this world, will have no defender on the Day of Resurrection, where only Allah's knowledge of their secrets will matter.
