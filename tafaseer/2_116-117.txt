They say that God has taken a son, but He is declared utterly free of this claim, because everything in the heavens and the earth belongs to Him and all created beings are His servants under His ownership and authority. A son only comes from comparable beings through a wife, and He has no equal, no rival, and no spouse. He is the Originator of the heavens and the earth, bringing them into existence when nothing like them existed before. All that exists is subject to Him in submission and obedience, whether willingly or by compulsion, and all will stand before Him alone. When He decrees a matter, He does not act through effort or process; He only commands it to be, and it comes into existence immediately. By this same command, Jesus was created directly by God’s will without a father, just as Adam was created by command without parents. This demonstrates that creation by divine command does not imply sonship, but complete power and ownership over all creation.
