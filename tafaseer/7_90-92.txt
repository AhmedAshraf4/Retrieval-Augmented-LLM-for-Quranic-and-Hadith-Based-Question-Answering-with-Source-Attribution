Tafsir Explanation: The people who rejected Shuayb were struck by a severe punishment after threatening to expel him and his followers. An earthquake shook them, causing them to lie dead and prostrate in their homes. Later, Allah described their end in Surah Hud, where He saved Shuayb and the believers with him by His mercy, while the wrongdoers were seized by a loud cry, which also caused them to fall dead. In Surah Ash-Shuara', their mocking challenge to Shuayb was met with a punishment from a fiery cloud followed by a tremor and a deadly cry. The torment left their bodies lifeless, making it as if they had never lived in the land they had inhabited. This destruction confirmed their earlier loss as those who opposed Shu`ayb.
