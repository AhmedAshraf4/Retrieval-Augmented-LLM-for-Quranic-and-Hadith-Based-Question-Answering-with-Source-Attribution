Tafsir Explanation: The Jews envied the Muslims and plotted to mislead them, wishing to make them stray from the faith. They concealed the truth in their scriptures about the Prophet Muhammad and mixed it with falsehood. Some of them pretended to believe in the morning, attending the Muslim prayer, but by evening, they reverted to their old religion to deceive the weak in faith, hoping to make them think there were flaws in Islam. They also advised each other not to trust anyone outside their faith, fearing that sharing their knowledge would lead Muslims to expose the truth and use it against them. Allah responded by declaring that true guidance comes from Him alone and that He grants His favor to whomever He wills. He reminded them that the knowledge and blessings they had were in His control and that He guides or misguides according to His will. He also emphasized that He had chosen to bless the Muslims with superior knowledge, having honored Prophet Muhammad over all others, and guided them to the best law.
