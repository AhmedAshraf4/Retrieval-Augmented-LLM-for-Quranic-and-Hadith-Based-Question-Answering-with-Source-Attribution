At a decisive moment, Allah commanded Musa to throw his stick, which immediately turned into a snake that swallowed all the ropes and sticks thrown by the magicians. This act revealed the truth, as the magicians realized this was a divine sign, not magic. They fell in prostration and declared belief in the Lord of Musa and Harun. The stick returned to its original form after consuming the magicians' illusions. The magicians acknowledged that had Musa been a magician, he would not have overcome them, and they submitted to the power of Allah.
