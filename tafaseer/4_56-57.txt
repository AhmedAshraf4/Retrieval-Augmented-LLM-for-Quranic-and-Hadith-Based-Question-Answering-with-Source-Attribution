Allah describes the punishment for those who reject His signs and hinder His messengers. These disbelievers will be cast into the Fire, which will burn their skins repeatedly. As their skin is burned, Allah will replace it with new skin so they can continue to feel the torment. This cycle will occur continuously, ensuring they experience unending pain. On the other hand, those who believe and perform righteous deeds will be admitted to Paradise, where rivers flow beneath them, and they will remain there forever. They will enjoy purified spouses, free from any bodily impurities or flaws, and they will reside in vast, ever-deepening shades. One of the trees in Paradise offers such immense shade that a rider traveling beneath it for a century would not reach its end.
