Tafsir Explanation: The Prophet told the people that a sign of Talut's kingship over them would be the return of the Tabut, the sacred wooden box taken from them. This box contained peace and reassurance from Allah, known as Sakinah. It also held remnants from what Moses and Aaron had left behind, including Moses' staff, the Tablets, and some mentioned the Torah. The angels carried the Tabut between the heavens and the earth and placed it before Talut while the people watched. This event affirmed the truth of the Prophet's message, confirming his prophethood and the command for the people to obey Talut, if they truly believed.
