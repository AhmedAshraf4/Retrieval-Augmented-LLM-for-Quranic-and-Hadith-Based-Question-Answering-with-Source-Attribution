Allah reassures the Prophet about the grief caused by his people's rejection of him, stating that their denial is not a personal affront to him but a rejection of Allah’s verses. Despite their defiance, Allah encourages patience, reminding the Prophet that previous messengers were also rejected and harmed by their people before Allah’s support granted them victory. Allah promises that His will cannot be altered, and the final victory belongs to the believers. The Prophet is advised not to despair over their refusal, as Allah could have guided them all if He willed, but only those destined to believe will do so. The disbelievers, described as spiritually dead, will be resurrected by Allah for judgment. The Prophet’s call will be heeded by those who understand, while the rest will remain in rejection until their final return to Allah for recompense.
