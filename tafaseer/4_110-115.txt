Allah emphasizes His forgiveness and mercy, assuring that anyone who commits evil or wrongs themselves, but seeks His forgiveness, will find Him forgiving and merciful. Even sins greater than the heavens, earth, and mountains can be forgiven if the sinner turns to Allah in repentance. Allah warns against falsely accusing innocent people, stating that those who do so bear a grave sin. He reminds the Prophet of His immense grace and mercy, without which some would have tried to mislead him, but their attempts would only harm themselves. Allah also highlights the value of reconciling between people, stating that such efforts, done seeking Allah’s pleasure, will be rewarded greatly. In contrast, those who oppose the Messenger after the truth has been made clear to them and follow a path other than that of the believers will be left to their chosen way and punished in Hell. Their hearts will be led astray, and they will find no escape from the punishment.
