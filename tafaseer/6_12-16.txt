Allah, the Creator and Sustainer of the heavens and earth, declares His ownership over everything within them and affirms that His mercy exceeds His anger. He has promised to gather all on the Day of Resurrection, a day with no doubt for the believers but uncertainty for the disbelievers. Everything in the night and day belongs to Allah, who alone is worthy of worship. He sustains His creation without needing anything in return. Allah instructs the Prophet Muhammad to reject the worship of anyone besides Allah, the Creator, and the One who feeds without being fed. Allah has commanded the Prophet to be the first to submit to Him and warns against becoming one of the idolaters. The success on that Day is to be saved from the torment and admitted into Paradise, a clear victory.
