Tafsir Explanation: Allah prohibits believers from marrying idol-worshipping women, excluding those from the People of the Scripture (Jews and Christians). The marriage to women from the People of the Scripture is allowed when the dowry is given and the intention is chastity. While some scholars view this as a restriction only for idolaters, the majority agree that it applies to both idolaters and People of the Scripture. The prohibition extends to marrying Mushrik men, as a believing man, even if a servant, is better than a Mushrik man, regardless of his wealth or status. The verse emphasizes that Mushriks invite to the Fire, while Allah invites to Paradise and forgiveness. Allah's clear signs are meant to remind people of the right path.
