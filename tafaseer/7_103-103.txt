After the previous prophets, Allah sent Moses with clear signs to Pharaoh, the ruler of Egypt, and his chiefs. Pharaoh and his people wrongfully rejected the signs, despite being convinced of their truth, out of arrogance and injustice. They denied the signs and caused corruption. As a result, Allah punished them by drowning them all, a disgraceful end that added to their suffering, while it served as a source of comfort for Moses and his believing followers, who witnessed the downfall of the tyrants.
