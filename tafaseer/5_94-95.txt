Tafsir Explanation: Allah tests His servants during Ihram by bringing wild game near them, making it easy to capture with their hands or spears, thus testing who fears Him in private and public. This test occurred during the Umrah of Al-Hudaybiyyah, when the Muslims encountered wild game like never before. Allah prohibited hunting these animals while in Ihram, except for five creatures (crow, kite, scorpion, mouse, and rabid dog) that may be killed. If someone kills game intentionally, they must offer an animal of equal value to what was killed as expiation, to be judged by two just men. If the animal cannot be found, they must feed the poor or fast, depending on the type of game. Allah will forgive past mistakes made during Jahiliyyah, but anyone who commits the offense again after knowing it is prohibited will face Allah's retribution.
