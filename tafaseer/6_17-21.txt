Allah is the Irresistible, the One who grants benefit or harm as He wills, with none able to resist His decisions. If He brings harm, only He can remove it, and if He brings good, He has full power over all things. Allah is in complete control, and all creatures are subject to His judgment. He is wise in all His actions and fully aware of all things, placing everything in its rightful place. The Prophet Muhammad's mission, as revealed in the Qur'an, is to warn those who hear it, and those who reject it, including the idolaters, will face the Fire. The People of the Book recognize the Prophet as they do their own children because previous messengers foretold his coming, his attributes, and his mission. Those who lie about Allah or deny His signs are the most unjust, and they will never succeed.
