Allah condemns the Jews for choosing misguidance and altering His words, as well as for mocking Islam. They ignored the guidance sent to Muhammad, deliberately distorting the inherited knowledge of previous Prophets to gain fleeting worldly benefits. They wished for the believers to go astray, rejecting the divine message. Allah, knowing their true nature, warns the believers against their enemies, assuring that He alone is their Protector and Helper. The Jews twisted words from their proper places, intentionally misinterpreting the divine message, and mockingly said, "We hear and disobey," showing their stubborn disbelief. They used the word "Ra`ina," which was a hidden insult, as a mockery of the Prophet. If they had instead said, "We hear and obey" and asked to be made to understand, it would have been better for them. However, due to their rejection, Allah cursed them for their disbelief, and only a few among them believe.
