On the Day of Resurrection, the disbelievers will witness the torment of the Fire and its restraints, and they will regret their disbelief, wishing to be sent back to the world to believe and perform righteous deeds. However, Allah reveals that their hidden disbelief will be made clear, showing they never truly desired to embrace faith but only sought escape from the torment. If they were returned to the world, they would again commit the same disbelief and defiance they were prohibited from, proving their earlier words to be lies. They will deny the Hereafter, claiming that this world is their only life and they will never be resurrected. When they stand before Allah, they will acknowledge the truth of Resurrection but will then be condemned for their denial, with Allah commanding them to taste the punishment for their rejection of the truth.
