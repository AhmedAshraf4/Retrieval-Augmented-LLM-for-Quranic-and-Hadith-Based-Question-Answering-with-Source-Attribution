The Children of Israel were commanded through Moses to slaughter a cow, and any cow would have fulfilled the command, but they persisted in questioning and hesitation, so the requirement became increasingly specific. They asked Moses to call upon Allah to clarify what kind of cow it was, and he conveyed that it was neither old nor young but of middle age, strong and mature. They continued to say that cows appeared similar to them, so they requested further description, and Moses conveyed that it was a bright yellow cow that pleased those who looked at it. They asked again, and he conveyed that it was not trained to plow the land or water fields and that it was free from any physical defect. After all these conditions were stated, they still showed reluctance, but they finally slaughtered the cow, coming close to not doing so because of their resistance and delay.
