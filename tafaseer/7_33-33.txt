Fahishah, sin, transgression, shirk, and lying about Allah are prohibited. Allah is more jealous than anyone, which is why He has prohibited both open and secret fahishah. He dislikes sin and oppression without justification. Ithm refers to disobedience, while oppression is when one transgresses against others. Both are prohibited. Allah also forbids associating partners with Him in worship, an act for which no authority has been given, and lying about Allah, such as falsely claiming that He has a son. These prohibitions emphasize avoiding idolatry and falsehoods about Allah.
