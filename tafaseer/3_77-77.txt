Tafsir Explanation: Those who prefer the fleeting pleasures of this life over fulfilling their covenant with Allah—by following Muhammad, confirming his truth, and announcing his description—will have no share in the Hereafter. Allah will neither speak to them nor look at them with mercy on the Day of Resurrection, nor will He purify them from their sins. Instead, they will be cast into the Fire to face a painful torment. The Prophet Muhammad detailed that three groups of people will suffer this fate: the one who lets their clothes hang below the ankles, the one who swears falsely to sell merchandise, and the one who gives charity only to remind others of it. Additionally, anyone who swears falsely to acquire someone else's property will meet Allah’s wrath.
