Tafsir Explanation: Allah commands His servants to spend from what He has granted them in His path before the Day of Resurrection arrives, a day when no one can bargain, form friendships, or seek intercession. On that Day, no one will be able to ransom themselves, even if they offer the earth's fill of gold. Neither kinship nor intercession will benefit anyone. The disbelievers, who reject Allah, will be the wrongdoers, as their disbelief will lead to their ultimate ruin on that Day.
