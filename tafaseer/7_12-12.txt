Tafsir Explanation: Iblis was asked why he did not prostrate when Allah commanded him to do so. Iblis responded that he was better than Adam, claiming that being created from fire made him superior to Adam, who was created from clay. This false comparison led Iblis to refuse Allah's command. Iblis disregarded the honor Allah bestowed upon Adam, such as creating him with His own Hand and blowing life into him. In his arrogance, Iblis made a flawed analogy, placing greater value on fire, which represents recklessness, over clay, which symbolizes qualities like patience and growth. As a result, Iblis was cast out from Allah's mercy. His refusal to submit was the first act of using analogy (Qiyas) incorrectly. In contrast, Adam's creation from clay led him to repent, humble himself, and seek Allah's forgiveness.
