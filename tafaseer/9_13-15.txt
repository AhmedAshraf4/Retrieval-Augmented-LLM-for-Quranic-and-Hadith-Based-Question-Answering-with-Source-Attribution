These verses encourage the believers to fight against the idolators who broke their covenants, including those who attempted to expel the Messenger from Makkah. The idolators initially came to protect their caravan, but when it escaped, they pursued fighting the Muslims out of arrogance. They also broke their peace agreement with the Muslims and aided their allies in attacking the Khuza'ah tribe. In response, the Messenger of Allah marched to Makkah and conquered it. Allah commands the believers to fear Him instead of the idolators, as He is the ultimate authority. The believers are ordered to fight, as Allah will punish the disbelievers by their hands, disgrace them, and grant victory to the believers. This battle will also heal the anger of the believers’ hearts, and Allah will accept the repentance of those He wills, showing His wisdom and knowledge in His actions.
