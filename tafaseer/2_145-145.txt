The Jews displayed disbelief and stubbornness, refusing to obey the Prophet or abandon their desires even if every proof of his truth came to them. Allah emphasizes that bringing all signs to the People of the Scripture would not make them follow the Prophet’s Qiblah. The Prophet does not follow their Qiblah because he strictly obeys Allah’s commands, not their desires, and he would never conform to their opinions. Praying toward Bayt Al-Maqdis was commanded by Allah, not adopted from the Jews. Allah warns that knowingly following their desires after receiving knowledge would make one a wrongdoer, highlighting the stronger accountability for those who reject clear proof.
