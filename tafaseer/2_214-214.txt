Victory comes after enduring trials, as Allah tests His believers just as He tested those before them. The people of previous nations faced severe hardships such as poverty, illness, and disasters. They were so shaken by these trials that even the Prophet and his followers in the Battle of the Confederates feared for their lives. Despite the challenges, the true believers endured, and Allah promised that victory would come, just as the Prophet Muhammad's struggle saw both losses and gains. Allah’s promise of aid is certain, and relief follows hardship. This pattern of testing and eventual success is a clear sign of the truth of faith.
