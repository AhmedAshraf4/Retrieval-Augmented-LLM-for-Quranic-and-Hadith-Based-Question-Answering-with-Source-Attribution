Allah commands His Messenger and the believers to follow the revelation sent to him, to obey it, and to act according to it. What has been revealed is the undeniable Truth, and there is no god worthy of worship except Allah. He instructs Muhammad to turn aside from the idolaters, meaning to forgive them, be patient, and endure their harm until Allah grants him victory over them. Allah's will is behind the misleading of the idolaters, and had He willed, they would not have taken others as deities. Allah’s will and wisdom in all matters are perfect, and He is not questioned about His decisions, while they will be held accountable. Muhammad has not been made responsible for watching over their deeds or controlling their affairs, but only to deliver the message, as Allah has commanded him to remind, not to dominate.
