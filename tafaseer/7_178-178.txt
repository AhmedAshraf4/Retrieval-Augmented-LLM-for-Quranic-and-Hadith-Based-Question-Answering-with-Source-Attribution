Tafsir Explanation: Allah alone deserves all praise, and He is the one from whom help, guidance, and forgiveness are sought. Humans are urged to seek refuge with Allah from the evils within themselves and the consequences of their misdeeds. The one who is guided by Allah will never be led astray, while the one whom Allah misguides will have no one to guide them. This statement affirms the absolute oneness of Allah, declaring that there is no deity worthy of worship except Him, and that Muhammad is His servant and Messenger.
