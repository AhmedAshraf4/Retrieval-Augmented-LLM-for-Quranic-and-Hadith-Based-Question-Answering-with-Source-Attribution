The Children of Israel said to Moses that they would not believe until they saw Allah openly, demanding direct sight while they were gathered. A bolt of lightning struck them, causing death while some were watching. Moses stood supplicating, fearing what he would say to the Children of Israel after the destruction of those chosen men. Allah informed him that these men had been among those who worshipped the calf. Allah then restored them to life one by one while the others watched their resurrection, returning them after death so they could complete their lives and be grateful. This occurred after Moses had returned with the Tablets of the Law, which they refused to accept unless Allah appeared and spoke to them directly. After their death by the thunderbolt, Allah revived them and again commanded them to take the Book, demonstrating that they were restored to life after punishment for their demand to see Allah plainly.
