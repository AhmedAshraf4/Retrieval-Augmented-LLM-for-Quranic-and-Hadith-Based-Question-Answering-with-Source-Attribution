When the polytheists are called to follow the guidance revealed to the Messenger of Allah and abandon their misguided practices, they reject it, saying they will follow what they found their fathers following, specifically idol worship and false deities. Allah criticizes their reasoning, highlighting that their forefathers had no understanding or guidance. The disbelievers are likened to animals that do not understand what is being said to them; they are deaf, dumb, and blind, unable to hear, speak, or see the truth. They are compared to wandering animals that cannot comprehend the benefit of what is being presented to them.
