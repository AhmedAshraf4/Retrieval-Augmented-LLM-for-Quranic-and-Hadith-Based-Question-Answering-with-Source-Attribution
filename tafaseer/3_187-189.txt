Tafsir Explanation: Allah reproaches the People of the Scriptures for breaking their covenant, which required them to acknowledge Muhammad and make his identity clear to others. Instead, they concealed the truth and chose fleeting material gain over the promised rewards of this world and the Hereafter, making a disastrous choice. This serves as a warning to scholars, urging them to openly share beneficial knowledge and avoid withholding it. Those who boast about what they have not done, or seek praise for unearned actions, will face punishment. The example of such people includes hypocrites who stayed behind during battles, seeking praise for false excuses. Allah assures that no one can escape His punishment, for He holds dominion over the heavens and earth and is capable of all things.
