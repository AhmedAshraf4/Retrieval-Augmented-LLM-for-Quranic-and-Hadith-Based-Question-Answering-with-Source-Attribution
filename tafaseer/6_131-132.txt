Tafsir Explanation: Allah does not destroy the populations of towns for their wrongdoing while their people are unaware. This is because He sends Messengers and reveals Books to both Jinn and mankind, ensuring no one can claim ignorance before being punished for their wrongs. Allah did not punish any nation without first sending a warner to them, as stated in various verses. Every nation has been sent a Messenger to call them to worship Allah and avoid false deities. Allah's punishment only comes after a clear warning has been delivered. Each person is rewarded or punished based on their actions, and the disbelievers will receive degrees of torment in the Fire according to their evil deeds. Allah is fully aware of all actions and records them to recompense individuals when they return to Him.
