Maryam was accepted by Allah due to her mother's vow, and Allah made her grow in a good manner, making her conduct delightful and well-liked by people. She was placed under the care of Zakariyya, who was her guardian and taught her righteousness, knowledge, and religion. Zakariyya, who was married to her maternal aunt, served as her protector, and Maryam was raised with righteous individuals. At the place of worship, every time Zakariyya entered the Mihrab, he found Maryam supplied with sustenance that was out of season. He asked her where it came from, and she replied that it was from Allah, who provides sustenance to whom He wills without limit.
