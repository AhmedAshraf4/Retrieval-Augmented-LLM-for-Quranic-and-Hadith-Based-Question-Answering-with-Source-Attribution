The scholars of the People of the Scripture recognized the truth of Muhammad as clearly as a man recognizes his own child, a parable for something evident. They knew from their Books that he was the Messenger, yet they concealed the truth from the people. Abdullah bin Salam testified that he recognized Muhammad as the Honest One described in the Scriptures, even without knowing his mother’s story. Despite this knowledge and certainty, they hid the truth, but Allah affirmed to the Prophet and the believers that his message is the truth from the Lord and that they should not doubt it.
