Tafsir Explanation: Allah revealed His message to Prophet Muhammad just as He had done with previous prophets, including Noah, Abraham, Moses, and David, among others. The Quran mentions 25 prophets by name, including Adam, Noah, Abraham, Moses, and Jesus, with Muhammad being the last. Allah spoke directly to Moses, an honor that made him known as "the one whom Allah spoke to." Prophets were sent as bearers of both good news and warnings, guiding people to obey Allah and avoid His punishment. This was done to ensure that humanity would have no excuse before Allah for their disobedience. If people had been destroyed before receiving the message, they would have claimed that they needed a prophet to follow. Allah sent prophets and books to prevent such excuses and establish His wisdom and power, ensuring that no one could plead ignorance.
