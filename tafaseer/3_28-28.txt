Tafsir Explanation: Allah prohibited the believers from taking disbelievers as allies or showing affection towards them instead of fellow believers. Anyone who engages in this behavior will be forsaken by Allah. Allah warns that those who befriend disbelievers instead of believers are straying from the straight path. He also clarifies that disbelievers are allies to one another, and if the believers do not follow this distinction, it will lead to widespread corruption and mischief on earth. However, if a believer fears harm from the disbelievers, they may outwardly show friendship for safety, but their hearts should remain firm in loyalty to the believers. Allah cautions against His anger for those who support His enemies and assures that each individual’s deeds will be judged by Him in the end.
