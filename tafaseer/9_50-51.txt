Allah highlights the hypocrisy of those who rejoice when the Prophet and his Companions receive blessings, such as victory, but feel distressed when calamity strikes, claiming they had already taken precautions by not joining him. They turn away, pleased with their actions. Allah instructs the Prophet to respond by declaring that nothing will happen to them except what Allah has ordained, for He is their protector and master. Believers are urged to place their trust in Allah, as He is sufficient and the best guardian.
