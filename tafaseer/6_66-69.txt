Tafsir Explanation: The Quraysh denied the truth of the Qur'an and the guidance that Muhammad brought, despite it being the ultimate truth. Allah instructed Muhammad to clarify that he was not responsible for their actions and that his duty was only to deliver the message. Those who follow him will find happiness, while those who reject him will face misery. Every matter has a set time to occur, and the truth of the message will become clear eventually. Allah warned against sitting with those who mock or distort His verses, advising to avoid them until they change the subject. If one forgets and sits with them, they are to leave once they remember, as agreeing with them would make them complicit. Believers who avoid such wrongdoers are not accountable for them, but are reminded to warn them in hopes they may turn from their error.
