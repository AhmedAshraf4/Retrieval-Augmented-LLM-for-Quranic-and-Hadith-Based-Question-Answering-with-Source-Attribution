Tafsīr Explanation: The Quraysh and some Jews denied that Allah had sent any revelation to a human, questioning the legitimacy of a human messenger. Allah responded by pointing to the Torah sent to Moses as a light and guidance, which the people had altered, hiding parts of it. The Qur'an, revealed to Muhammad, is similarly a blessed book, confirming earlier scriptures and warning the people of Makkah and beyond. The Qur'an reveals knowledge of past and future events, which neither the Arabs nor their ancestors knew. Those who believe in the Last Day accept the Qur'an, and they are diligent in their prayers. Muhammad, unlike previous prophets sent to specific nations, was sent to all of humanity.
