If a wife fears that her husband may desert her, she can offer to forgo some of her rights, like provisions or housing, and the husband may accept this. This peaceful settlement is considered better than divorce, as it avoids separation. The Prophet accepted a similar arrangement when Sawdah gave up her day to A’ishah, fearing divorce. Making peace is preferred over divorce, and if a husband treats a wife he dislikes justly, Allah will reward him. It is impossible to achieve perfect justice between wives, as love and desire can never be equally divided. A husband must not excessively favor one wife, leaving the others in an uncertain state, as this is unjust. If a husband leans too much toward one, he will face consequences on the Day of Judgment. However, if spouses divorce, Allah will provide for each from His bounty, as He is All-Sufficient and Wise in His decisions.
