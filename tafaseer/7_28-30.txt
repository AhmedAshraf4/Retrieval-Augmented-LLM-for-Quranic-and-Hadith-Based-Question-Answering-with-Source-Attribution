Tafsīr Explanation: The idolators performed Tawaf around the Ka'bah naked, claiming that their forefathers had done the same, and that it was commanded by Allah. Women also participated, covering only their private parts, saying that whatever was exposed was not for men’s enjoyment. Allah refuted this practice, stating that He does not command such indecency, but rather justice and sincerity in worship. He ordered that people should face Him in every mosque, worshipping Him sincerely. Allah then reminded them that, as He created them from nothing, He will resurrect them again after death. He created mankind, some as believers and others as disbelievers, and those who are guided will act in accordance with their fate. Those who chose to follow the devils instead of Allah will be misguided, and their deviation will be punished.
