Tafsir Explanation: Allah sent the Qur'an as a clear proof to His creation to eliminate excuses. People might claim that if a Book had been revealed to them, like it was to the Jews and Christians, they would have been more guided. However, Allah refutes this by stating that the Qur'an is the guidance and mercy sent to them. The people previously swore that if a warner came to them, they would be more guided, but the Qur'an, revealed to Prophet Muhammad, was already sent with clear proofs and guidance. Those who reject the Ayat of Allah or turn away from them are the most unjust, as they not only reject the message but also discourage others from following it.
