Tafsir Explanation: Allah destroyed many towns that defied His Messengers, rejecting them and earning disgrace both in this world and the Hereafter. Their punishment came suddenly, either at night or while they were resting during the day, times of leisure and heedlessness. They admitted their wrongdoings only when the torment struck, saying they were wrongdoers. On the Day of Resurrection, Allah will question the people to whom the Messages were sent, as well as the Messengers themselves, about how they responded to the divine call. Everything will be disclosed with full knowledge, as nothing escapes Allah’s observation, not even the smallest details, which are all recorded in a clear book.
