The creation of the heavens and earth, with their vastness, structure, and celestial bodies, provides clear signs of Allah’s power and unity. The alternation of night and day, which follow each other without delay, showcases His control over time and change. The length of day and night fluctuates, with one merging into the other, demonstrating the perfect balance Allah maintains. Allah has shaped the seas to carry ships, benefiting humanity by enabling trade and transport. He sends rain from the sky, bringing life to the earth, similar to how He revives barren land with crops. The creatures scattered across the earth, in various forms and sizes, are all sustained by Allah, who knows their every movement and provision. The winds, which bring mercy or torment, guide the clouds across the earth, fulfilling Allah’s will. These natural phenomena—heavens, earth, day and night, seas, creatures, winds, and clouds—serve as signs of Allah’s Oneness for those who reflect. Such signs are reminders for the mindful to acknowledge Allah’s creation and seek His mercy.
