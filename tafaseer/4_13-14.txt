Tafsir Explanation: The limits set by Allah regarding inheritance must not be violated, as they are His ordained boundaries based on the relationship and dependency of the heirs. Those who obey Allah and His Messenger, fulfilling the inheritance shares as prescribed, will be rewarded with eternal Paradise, where rivers flow beneath them. In contrast, those who disobey and transgress these limits, altering the shares and disputing Allah's judgment, will face eternal punishment in the Fire, suffering disgraceful torment. This is illustrated by the hadith about a person who might live righteously for many years but changes their will unfairly, leading to their downfall, while another who might have lived sinfully may find redemption through fairness in their will. The importance of adhering to Allah's rules in matters of inheritance is emphasized as the ultimate determinant of success or failure in the Hereafter.
