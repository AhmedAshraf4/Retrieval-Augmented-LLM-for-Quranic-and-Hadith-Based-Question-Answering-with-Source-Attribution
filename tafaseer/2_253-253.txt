Allah has honored some Prophets above others, as reflected in His statement that He gave certain Prophets higher status, such as speaking directly to Moses and Muhammad. In the event of a dispute between a Muslim and a Jew, the Prophet Muhammad clarified that no Prophet should be preferred above another, emphasizing that the superiority of the Prophets is Allah’s decision alone. The Prophet Muhammad explained that on the Day of Resurrection, he would be the first to be resurrected but would find Moses already holding onto the Throne, unsure if Moses had recovered before him or was still unconscious from the Trumpet's blast. Allah also gave Jesus clear signs and supported him with the Spirit of Purity, referring to the angel Gabriel. After these clear signs were given to the Children of Israel, they still differed, with some believing and others disbelieving, though Allah’s will determined the ensuing conflict.
