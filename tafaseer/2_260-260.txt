Tafsir Explanation: Ibrahim requested from Allah to show him how the dead are resurrected, seeking to strengthen his faith by witnessing the act with his own eyes. Allah instructed him to take four birds, slaughter and cut them into pieces, then place the pieces on separate hills. When Ibrahim called the birds, their parts flew back together, and the birds were resurrected, walking toward him. Each bird sought its own head from his hand, refusing any other. This miraculous event demonstrated Allah's power and wisdom, showing that He can bring about anything by His will. Allah’s response highlighted His supreme might and wisdom.
