Allah created humanity from a single soul, Adam, and from him, He created his mate, Hawwa. From them both, many men and women were spread across the earth. Allah sends down rain from the sky in measured amounts, which nourishes the earth, bringing forth vegetation of all kinds. This includes green plants, fruits, and clustered grains, as well as dates and grapes, which are highly valued, especially in the Arabian region. The date-palm produces clusters of dates, hanging low for easy harvesting. The earth also grows olives and pomegranates, similar in appearance yet distinct in taste and form. Allah encourages people to observe the fruits when they ripen, reflecting on His ability to transform dry wood into fruitful trees, each with unique qualities. In the earth, various plants grow from the same water, yet some are better than others in terms of taste, demonstrating Allah’s wisdom and mercy. These are signs for those who believe and recognize His power.
