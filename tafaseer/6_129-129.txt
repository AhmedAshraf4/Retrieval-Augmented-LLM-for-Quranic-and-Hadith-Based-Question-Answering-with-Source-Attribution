Allah makes the wrongdoers supporters of each other, leading them into the Fire. He appoints the wrongdoers of the Jinn to be over the wrongdoers of mankind. This connection ensures that the wrongdoers are tested and destroyed by one another. Just as Allah made the misguided humans and Jinn allies, He uses the wrongdoers to bring about mutual destruction, with each group being a cause for the other’s ruin. This is a just recompense for their transgression.
