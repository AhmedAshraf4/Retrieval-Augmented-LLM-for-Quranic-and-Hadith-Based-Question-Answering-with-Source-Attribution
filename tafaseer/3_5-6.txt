Allah has complete knowledge of everything in the heavens and earth, and nothing is hidden from Him. He shapes His creations in the wombs as He wills, deciding whether one will be male or female, handsome or otherwise, happy or miserable. He is the only true deity worthy of worship, the Almighty and All-Wise. This verse emphasizes that Jesus, son of Mary, is a created servant of Allah, just like all humans. Allah created Jesus in the womb and shaped him as He willed. The claim that Jesus is divine is refuted, as he, like all humans, underwent creation in stages, just as Allah described in another verse.
