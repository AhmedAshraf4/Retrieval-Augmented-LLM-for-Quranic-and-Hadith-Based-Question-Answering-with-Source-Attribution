Allah warns the Children of Israel to fear the Day of Resurrection, when no person will benefit another and no soul will carry the burden of another. On that Day, neither a father nor a son will help the other, and every person will be occupied only with their own outcome. For those who disbelieve, no intercession will be accepted, no ransom will be taken, and no compensation will be received, even if they offered the earth filled with gold. Family ties, loyalty, friendship, masters, or false gods will give no protection, and none will feel anger or concern to defend them from punishment. No helper will arise, no one will rescue them, and no refuge will exist against Allah. They will stand surrendered and helpless, with all former support cut off, while judgment belongs solely to Allah, who cannot be resisted, ransomed, or opposed.
