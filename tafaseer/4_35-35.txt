Tafsir Explanation: When estrangement occurs between a husband and wife, Allah commands the appointment of two trustworthy arbitrators, one from each family's side, to assess the situation. The arbitrators' role is to determine whether reconciliation is possible or if separation is necessary. If both parties desire peace, Allah will guide them to reconcile. If one party is at fault, the arbitrators may intervene, either guiding the wronged party to make restitution or ensuring the marriage remains intact. The decision made by the arbitrators is binding, even if the spouses did not appoint them. If the marriage continues, but one disagrees, the one who agrees will inherit from the other if they pass away.
