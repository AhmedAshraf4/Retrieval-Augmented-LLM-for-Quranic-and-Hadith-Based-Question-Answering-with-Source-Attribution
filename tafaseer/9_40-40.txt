Allah reassures the Prophet that even if his supporters fail to help him, Allah will support and protect him as He did when the disbelievers drove him out. During the Hijrah, the idolaters sought to kill, imprison, or exile the Prophet, but he escaped with Abu Bakr to the cave of Thawr, where they remained for three days. While hiding, Abu Bakr feared the disbelievers might discover them, but the Prophet reassured him by saying, "What do you think about two, with Allah as their third?" Allah then sent down His tranquility and aided them with invisible forces, possibly referring to angels. Allah made the disbelievers' words inferior while His Word, "La ilaha illallah," became supreme. The Prophet said that those who fight to make Allah's Word superior are fighting in His cause. Allah is described as All-Mighty in His retribution and All-Wise in His actions.
