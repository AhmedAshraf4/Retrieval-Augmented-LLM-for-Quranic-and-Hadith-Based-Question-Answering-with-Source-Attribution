Allah forbids believers from adopting the disbelievers' false belief that those who die in battle or during travel could have avoided death if they had stayed behind. The disbelievers claim that if their brethren had remained in their lands instead of traveling or going to fight, they would not have died or been killed. Allah refutes this, stating that life and death are in His hands alone, and no one dies or lives except by His decree. He causes their regret and grief, making them feel the loss more acutely. Allah emphasizes that martyrdom or dying in His cause is far better, as it leads to His forgiveness and mercy, which surpasses all worldly gains. Whether one dies or is killed, they will be gathered to Allah, where they will be judged according to their deeds.
