Tafsir Explanation: Every nation has an appointed term, and when it arrives, they cannot delay or hasten it by even an hour. Allah sent Messengers to the Children of Adam, delivering His signs with both good news and warnings. Those who fear Allah and act righteously, avoiding prohibitions and performing acts of obedience, will have no fear or grief. However, those who deny His signs and arrogantly reject them will dwell in the Fire forever, with no end to their punishment.
