Tafsir Explanation: If a husband divorces his wife before the marriage is consummated, he must give her half of the agreed-upon Mahr. This is the agreed-upon practice according to scholars. However, the wife may choose to forgo her right to this half of the Mahr, releasing the husband from further financial responsibility. Alternatively, the husband may choose to forgo the Mahr entirely. The one who remits the Mahr is considered closer to piety. Allah advises both parties to be generous and not forget kindness in their dealings with each other. Allah is fully aware of all actions and will reward everyone accordingly.
