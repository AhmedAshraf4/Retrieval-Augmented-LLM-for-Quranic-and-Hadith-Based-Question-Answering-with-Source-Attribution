Umm Salamah asked the Prophet Muhammad about women’s lesser share in inheritance and their lack of participation in battle compared to men. In response, Allah revealed that people should not wish for the benefits others have been given, as each will receive their due reward based on their actions. Men and women each earn the reward for their deeds, whether good or bad. This applies to inheritance as well, where everyone receives their rightful share. The verse encourages asking Allah for His bounty instead of desiring what others have. Allah, being All-Knowing, understands who deserves what in this life and the Hereafter, granting each person what is best for them based on His wisdom.
