Tafsir Explanation: Allah guides those who follow what pleases Him to the paths of peace, which refers to Islam or Paradise. He saves the believers from the darkness of disbelief, doubt, and confusion, bringing them into the light of clear and undeniable truth. In contrast, Shaytan leads the disbelievers, making the paths of ignorance and misguidance appear appealing, which leads them away from the true path into disbelief and wickedness. The singular mention of light signifies the singularity of truth, while the plural mention of darkness highlights the many false forms of disbelief. This is consistent with other verses where truth is singular and falsehood has many divisions and paths, all of which deviate from the straight path ordained by Allah.
