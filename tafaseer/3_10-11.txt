On the Day of Resurrection, the disbelievers’ wealth and offspring will not protect them from Allah’s punishment. Despite their material possessions in this life, these will not save them from His severe torment. They will be the fuel for the Fire, akin to the fate of the people of Pharaoh, who rejected Allah’s signs and messengers. The disbelievers will face the same end as those who denied the truth before them, perishing and being punished in Hell. Allah’s punishment is severe, and no one can escape His grasp. His will prevails over all, and He alone is the true Lord.
