Tafsir Explanation: Allah informed Maryam that Isa would be taught the Book and Al-Hikmah, referring to the Torah and the Injil, which Isa memorized. Allah appointed him as a Messenger to the Children of Israel, where he performed miracles to prove his divine mission, including shaping a bird from clay, breathing into it, and making it come to life, healing the blind and leprous, and bringing the dead to life. These miracles, especially the healing of the blind and leprous, were extraordinary challenges in a time when medicine was advancing. `Isa also informed the people of things they had eaten and stored in their homes, showing his knowledge of the unseen. He confirmed the truth of the Torah and made lawful what was previously forbidden, addressing Jewish disputes. He urged them to fear Allah, obey him, and worship only Allah, as he and they were equally servants of Allah, guiding them to the straight path.
