Allah takes the souls of His servants both at death and during sleep, which is considered a minor form of death. He controls all actions, knowing everything done by day and night. The soul is taken during sleep and returned when the person wakes up, fulfilling the appointed lifespan. When death approaches, the angels, including the Angel of Death, take the soul, ensuring no neglect of their duties. The soul of a righteous person is welcomed with honor, while the soul of the wicked is rejected, and its return is marked by disgrace. All souls are ultimately returned to Allah, their Just Lord, for judgment on the Day of Resurrection, when no one will be wronged.
