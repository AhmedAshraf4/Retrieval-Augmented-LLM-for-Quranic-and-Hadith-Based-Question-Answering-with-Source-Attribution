Allah warns not to be deceived by the temporary enjoyment of disbelievers, as their pleasures are short-lived and will lead to torment in Hell. While they may appear to prosper in this world, their ultimate destination is the Fire, where they will find a terrible resting place. This is similar to other verses that emphasize the briefness of worldly delights for the disbelievers, followed by severe punishment. In contrast, those who fear their Lord will be rewarded with eternal gardens beneath which rivers flow, a permanent residence granted by Allah. For the righteous, what is with Allah is far better than the fleeting pleasures of this life.
