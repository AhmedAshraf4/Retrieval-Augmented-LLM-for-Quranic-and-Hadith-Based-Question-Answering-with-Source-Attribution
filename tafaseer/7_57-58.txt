Allah sends the wind to carry heavy clouds filled with rain, driving them to dry, lifeless lands. When the rain falls, it brings forth fruits, just as Allah will resurrect the dead on the Day of Resurrection. This is compared to the revival of the earth after it has been dead. The good land, receiving rain, produces vegetation easily, while bad land struggles to produce anything. Similarly, knowledge and guidance from Allah, like rain, benefit those who accept and understand it, producing abundant results. Those who reject it, however, gain nothing. The comparison highlights the effect of divine mercy and resurrection.
