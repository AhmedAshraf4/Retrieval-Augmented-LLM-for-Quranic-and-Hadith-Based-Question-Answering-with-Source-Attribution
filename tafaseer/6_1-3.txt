Surat Al-An`am was revealed in Makkah at night, accompanied by seventy thousand angels, who glorified Allah. Allah praises Himself for creating the heavens and earth as a home for His servants, and for providing the benefits of darkness and light. The plural form of darkness (Zulumat) contrasts with the singular form of light (An-Nur), indicating the higher honor of light. Allah created mankind from clay, starting with Adam, and decreed their term of life on earth, as well as their appointed end in the Hereafter. Death is the earthly term, and the final judgment is known only to Allah. He knows all things, whether hidden or revealed, and is the sole God of both the heavens and the earth. He is aware of everything humans earn through their actions.
