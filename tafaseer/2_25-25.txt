Those who believe and perform righteous deeds are given glad tidings of Gardens with rivers flowing beneath their trees and dwellings. The rivers do not run in valleys but emerge from beneath hills or mountains of musk, and the land and structures of Paradise are made of pearls, jewels, and fragrant substances. Whenever the people of Paradise are provided with fruit, they say it resembles what they were given before, because the fruits appear similar to one another and resemble what is known in this world, yet their taste is different and superior, with similarity only in appearance or name. They are granted purified spouses, free from physical impurities, bodily functions, menstruation, pregnancy, and sin. They remain in Paradise forever, with unending enjoyment and no death or interruption.
