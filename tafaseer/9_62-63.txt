Tafsir Explanation: A hypocrite, intending to please others, swore by Allah that the leaders were better than Muhammad's teachings. A Muslim overheard this and responded, affirming the truth of Muhammad's words. The Muslim reported the incident to the Prophet, who confronted the hypocrite, but he denied it, swearing falsely. The Muslim prayed for Allah to expose the liar. Allah then revealed that those who oppose Allah and His Messenger will face eternal punishment in Hell, experiencing disgrace and humiliation.
