In this Ayah, Allah asserts His absolute Oneness, stating that He is the only deity with no partners or equals. He is described as Allah, the One and Only, the Sustainer, with no other god deserving of worship. His Names, Al-Rahman (the Most Gracious) and Al-Rahim (the Most Merciful), emphasize His mercy. Allah further points to His creation of the heavens, the earth, and all creatures between them as proof of His singular divinity. These creations bear testimony to His Oneness, fulfilling His claim as the sole deity. The verse also highlights Allah’s Greatest Name, mentioned through the affirmation of His exclusive right to be worshipped.
