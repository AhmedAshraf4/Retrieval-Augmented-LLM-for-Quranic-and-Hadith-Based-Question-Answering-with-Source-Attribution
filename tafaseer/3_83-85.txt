Tafsir Explanation: Allah criticizes those who follow religions other than the one He revealed, which is the worship of Him alone. All creatures in the heavens and earth, whether willingly or unwillingly, are in submission to Allah’s will. This submission is not limited to believers, but extends to disbelievers who are under His unavoidable power. Allah’s dominion is absolute, and all creation, including moving creatures and angels, prostrate to Him, acknowledging His supremacy. Believers submit to Allah both in heart and body, while disbelievers submit only in body, being under His control. On the Day of Judgment, all will be returned to Allah, who will reward or punish them based on their deeds. Muslims believe in all the prophets and books Allah sent, from Ibrahim to Muhammad, without distinction, submitting to Allah alone. Anyone who seeks a religion other than Islam will find it rejected by Allah and will be a loser in the Hereafter.
