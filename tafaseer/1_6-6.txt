“Guide us to the straight path” is a request for Allah to direct, lead, and firmly establish the servant upon correct guidance that results in success. Guidance here includes being shown the way and being enabled to follow it. The straight path is a clear path without deviation, and it refers to Islam. The path is described through a concrete example: a straight road with protective boundaries, where open doors represent forbidden acts, remaining on the road represents obedience, and deviation occurs by entering what is prohibited. The Book of Allah calls people to stay on this path, while an inner admonition warns against crossing its limits. Even those already guided continue to ask for guidance because they require constant firmness, continuity, and protection from deviation. The request means remaining established on Islam, increasing in stability upon it, and not being turned away after guidance has been granted.
