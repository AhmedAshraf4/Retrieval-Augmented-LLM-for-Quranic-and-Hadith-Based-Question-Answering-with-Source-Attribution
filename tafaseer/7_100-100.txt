Allah questions whether the destruction of previous nations and the inheritance of their lands by others is not a lesson for those who follow. He warns that had He willed, He could have punished them for their sins just as He did to those before them. He made it clear that He could seal their hearts, preventing them from hearing guidance and advice. This reflects the fate of previous generations, whose dwellings remain as a reminder of their downfall. These past nations were destroyed for their disobedience, leaving only their abandoned homes as a sign for those who reflect. Yet, despite these clear signs, the people fail to listen and heed the lessons, showing a disregard for the warnings sent by Allah.
