Tafsir Explanation: Allah chose Musa above his people, speaking to him directly and giving him the Tablets, which contained commandments and detailed guidance. Allah commanded Musa to firmly adhere to what He had revealed and to instruct his people to follow the best of it. The Tablets contained advice, lawful and prohibited matters, and lessons. Musa was instructed to hold onto the message firmly, emphasizing obedience. Allah warned that those who defied His command would face destruction and loss. The Tablets were given to Musa as an enlightenment for mankind, and the teachings in them were meant to guide his people in their actions.
