Those who believe and perform righteous deeds will be granted eternal life in Paradise, as their hearts embrace faith and their actions align with it. Unlike the disbelievers, who reject Allah’s signs and are arrogant, these believers will not be burdened beyond their capacity. They will enter Paradise, where they will live forever, and Allah will cleanse them of any hatred or envy from their hearts. In Paradise, they will be purified and their faces will glow with delight. When the believers are saved from the Fire, they will pass a bridge between Paradise and Hell to settle any grievances from their worldly life. Once purified, they will be allowed to enter Paradise. Their reward in Paradise is not based solely on their deeds but on Allah's mercy, as even the Prophet Muhammad stated that no one will enter Paradise by their actions alone, but by Allah's grace. Those in Paradise will see their former place in Hell and be grateful for Allah’s guidance, while those in Hell will see their place in Paradise, filled with regret for not being guided.
