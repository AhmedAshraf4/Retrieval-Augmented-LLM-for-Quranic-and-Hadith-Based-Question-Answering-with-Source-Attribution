Tafsir Explanation: The straight path of your Lord is Islam, which Allah has revealed to Muhammad through the Qur'an. The Ayat are clear and detailed for those who understand the message of Allah and His Messenger. For them, there will be the abode of peace, which is Paradise, where they will be safe from all wickedness, because they followed the straight path. Allah, as their protector and helper, will reward them with Paradise due to their righteous actions.
