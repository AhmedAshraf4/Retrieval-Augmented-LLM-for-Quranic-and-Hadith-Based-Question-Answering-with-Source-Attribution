Those who killed their children and prohibited some of their wealth, based on their own innovations, have lost both in this life and the Hereafter. In this life, they lost when they unjustly killed their children and falsely restricted what Allah had provided. In the Hereafter, they will be in the worst dwellings, suffering severe torment for their lies and disbelief against Allah. Allah condemns those who invent lies about Him, warning that such individuals will not succeed, and they will face punishment for their disbelief when they return to Him.
