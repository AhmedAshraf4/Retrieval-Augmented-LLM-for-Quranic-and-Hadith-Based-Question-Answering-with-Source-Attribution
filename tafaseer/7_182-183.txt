Those who reject Our signs will be gradually seized in ways they do not perceive. The doors of provisions and means of livelihood will be opened to them in this life, deceiving them into thinking they are on the correct path. When they forget the warnings they had been given, Allah will open the gates to every pleasure for them. While they rejoice in what they have been granted, sudden punishment will strike, and they will be plunged into regret and sorrow. This leads to the cutting off of the wrongdoers. Allah’s plan is always strong and perfect, and He will grant them respite until their punishment arrives.
