Allah has provided mankind with Libas (clothing) to cover their private parts and Rish (adornment) for beautification. Libas is essential for modesty, while Rish is supplementary, including outer clothes and furniture. The "Libas of Taqwa" refers to the covering of a person's faults when they fear Allah, as their errors are concealed through piety. This emphasizes the importance of both physical coverings and the spiritual protection that comes from fearing Allah.
