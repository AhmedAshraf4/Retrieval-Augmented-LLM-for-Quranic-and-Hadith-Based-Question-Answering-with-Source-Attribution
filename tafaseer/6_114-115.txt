Tafsir Explanation: Allah asks whether He should seek a judge other than Himself, when He is the One who revealed the Book, fully explained, to the people. The Jews and Christians, who were given Scriptures, know that it was sent by the Lord in truth, as the earlier Prophets had foretold the coming of this message. Allah commands the Prophet not to doubt the truth of what has been revealed to him, as the truth has been established in full justice, and all of Allah's words are irrefutable. No one can change His decrees, whether in this life or the Hereafter, as Allah is the All-Hearer and the All-Knower of all actions, rewarding each according to their deeds.
