Ayat Al-Kursi is regarded as the greatest verse in the Qur'an, as confirmed by the Prophet, who praised it for its immense significance. It highlights Allah's unique sovereignty, asserting that He is the only deity, Ever-Living and Self-Sustaining. This verse describes Allah's perfect awareness, with no slumber or sleep overtaking Him, underscoring His absolute control over all creation. Everything in the heavens and earth belongs to Him, and no one can intercede except by His permission. Allah knows the past, present, and future of all beings, and His knowledge is beyond human comprehension. His Kursi extends over the heavens and the earth, and He is capable of preserving all things without fatigue. The verse concludes by affirming that Allah is the Most High and the Most Great. The verse’s significance is further emphasized by Hadiths, including those where the Prophet acknowledged the greatness of Ayat Al-Kursi and linked it to Allah’s Greatest Name, which can lead to protection from harm if recited regularly.
