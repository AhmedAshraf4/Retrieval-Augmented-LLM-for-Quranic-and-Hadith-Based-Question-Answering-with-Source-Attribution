Tafsir Explanation: People of different religions, including the People of the Scriptures, argued over whose Prophet and Book were superior. The People of the Scriptures claimed precedence because their Prophet came before the Prophet Muhammad and their Book before the Qur’an, while Muslims asserted their superiority due to Muhammad being the final Prophet and the Qur’an abrogating previous scriptures. Allah revealed that success is not determined by wishful thinking or mere claims, but by deeds. Those who work evil will face the consequences of their actions, while those who sincerely submit to Allah and do righteous deeds will be rewarded. Allah clarified that deeds must be done sincerely for Him and according to His guidance for them to be accepted. The Prophet Ibrahim is highlighted as an exemplary figure, being chosen as Allah’s intimate friend due to his unwavering obedience. Allah’s control over everything in the heavens and earth is emphasized, with His knowledge encompassing all things, leaving nothing hidden from His sight.
