Tafsir Explanation: The story of Uzayr centers on his observation of a ruined city, which was once a thriving civilization. After seeing its destruction, he questioned how Allah could bring it back to life. Allah caused him to die for a hundred years and then resurrected him, allowing him to witness the revival of his body and the city. His food and drink remained unchanged, showing Allah's power over time. His donkey's bones were scattered, but Allah gathered and clothed them with flesh, bringing it back to life. Witnessing these miracles, Uzayr declared his belief in Allah’s absolute power, affirming that He is capable of doing all things.
