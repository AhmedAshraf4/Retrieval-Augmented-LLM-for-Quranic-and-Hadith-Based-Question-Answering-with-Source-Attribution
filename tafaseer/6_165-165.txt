Allah has made mankind generations after generations, dwelling on earth as successors of one another. He has granted people different provisions, qualities, and ranks according to His wisdom. Some are raised above others in their worldly conditions to test how they will act with what He has given them. He tests the rich with their wealth and the poor with their patience. Allah has the power to replace humans with angels, but He has chosen to let them succeed one another on earth. This life is a test, and Allah observes how His creation behaves. He is swift in retribution for those who disobey, but He is also merciful and forgiving to those who follow His guidance. Allah’s mercy is vast, surpassing His anger, and He has created more mercy than wrath for His creation. The believer is reminded to be aware of the trials of this world, especially those related to desires such as women, as these are tests that have led many astray in the past. Those who obey and avoid what Allah has prohibited will be rewarded, while those who defy His commands will face swift punishment.
