The verse addresses a practice where people claimed that the milk in the bellies of certain cattle was exclusively for their males and prohibited for their females. They would slaughter male lambs and feed them only to the men, while female lambs, if born, were not slaughtered, and if stillborn, both men and women could share in eating them. This practice also extended to certain cattle like the Bahirah, where milk was restricted to men, and if the cattle died, both men and women would share the meat. Allah condemned this false attribution, stating that it was a fabrication to falsely declare certain things lawful or unlawful, a practice that was forbidden. Allah, being wise and all-knowing, will fully recompense those who engage in such deceitful actions.
