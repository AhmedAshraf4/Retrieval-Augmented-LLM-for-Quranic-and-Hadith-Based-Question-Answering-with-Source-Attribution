Shaytan deceived the idolators by convincing them to assign a portion of their crops and cattle to both Allah and their idols. He also made them believe it was right to kill their children out of fear of poverty and to bury their daughters alive to avoid dishonor. The devils led them to these actions, causing confusion in their religion and leading them to destruction. However, Allah's will allowed this to happen, and although He disapproves of these practices, He has perfect wisdom in His decrees. Allah does not need to be questioned about His actions, but the idolators will be questioned for theirs. Allah commands the believers to leave them to their fabrications, for He will judge between them and the idolators.
