Tafsir Explanation: Allah sent down angels to comfort and reassure the hearts of the believers, reminding them that victory comes solely from Him. He could have defeated the disbelievers without their involvement, but He allowed them to fight as a test. Those who are killed in His cause will not have their deeds lost; they will be guided, their state improved, and admitted into Paradise. The decision regarding the outcome of the battle belongs to Allah alone, and not to the Prophet, as Allah has complete authority over all matters. The Prophet is only tasked with delivering the message, and Allah guides whom He wills. Whether Allah pardons or punishes the disbelievers is entirely His decision. Allah holds sovereignty over everything in the heavens and the earth, and no one can question His actions. He forgives whom He wills and punishes whom He wills, for He is the All-Forgiving, Most Merciful.
