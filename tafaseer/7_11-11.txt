Tafsir Explanation: Allah created Adam from dried clay of altered mud, shaping him with His Hands and breathing life into him. He then commanded the angels to prostrate before Adam in honor of His creation. All the angels obeyed, except Iblis, who refused due to arrogance and envy. This event highlights Iblis's enmity toward Adam and his descendants. The ayah refers to Adam, the father of all mankind, and by extension, to his descendants. Allah also points out that just as He favored the forefathers of the Children of Israel, their descendants shared in that favor, but this is not the case with mankind's creation, as Adam was made from clay while his descendants are created from a different substance.
