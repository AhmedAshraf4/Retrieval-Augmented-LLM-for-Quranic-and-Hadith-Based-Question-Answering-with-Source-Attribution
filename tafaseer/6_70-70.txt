The verse commands to leave those who treat their religion as a game and are distracted by worldly life, as they will soon face severe punishment. People are reminded through the Qur'an to be aware of Allah's wrath and painful torment on the Day of Resurrection. It warns that no soul will escape the consequences of its actions, and that they will be exposed to destruction and denied access to good, prevented from attaining their desires. On the Day of Judgment, no protector or intercessor will be found for them, and even if they offer a ransom, it will not be accepted. Those who disbelieved and died in that state will not have their repentance accepted, even if they offer the earth full of gold. They will be given boiling water to drink and subjected to painful torment as a result of their disbelief.
