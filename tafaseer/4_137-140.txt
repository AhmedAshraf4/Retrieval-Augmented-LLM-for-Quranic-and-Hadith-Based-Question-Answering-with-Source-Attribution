Tafsir Explanation: Allah warns that those who embrace faith, then reject it, return to disbelief, and persist in it until death will never be granted repentance or guidance. The hypocrites, who support disbelievers in secret and mock the believers, are described as seeking honor with the disbelievers, although true honor belongs only to Allah, His Messenger, and the believers. Allah commands the faithful to avoid those who mock His verses, as sitting with them and approving of their denial or mockery is akin to sharing in their sin. The hypocrites, along with the disbelievers, will be gathered in Hell, where they will endure eternal punishment, bound in torment.
