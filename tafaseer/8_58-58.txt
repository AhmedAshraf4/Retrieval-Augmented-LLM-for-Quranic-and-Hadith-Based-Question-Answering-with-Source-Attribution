If you fear treachery from people with whom you have a peace treaty, you are to break the treaty and declare it null, ensuring both sides know the state of war exists. This action is necessary to maintain equality, as both parties will be aware that the agreement is no longer valid. Allah does not love those who betray, even if they are disbelievers. An example of this is when Muawiyah, during a valid peace treaty with the Romans, planned to approach their forces once the treaty ended in order to invade them. An old man warned him to avoid betrayal, and upon hearing the Prophet's directive on maintaining or nullifying such treaties only when the term expires, Muawiyah retreated.
