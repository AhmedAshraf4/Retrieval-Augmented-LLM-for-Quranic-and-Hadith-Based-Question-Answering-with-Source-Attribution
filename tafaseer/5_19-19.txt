Tafsir Explanation: The verse refers to the period that the People of the Book recognized as 300 years, with an additional 9 years to account for the difference between lunar and solar years. This period was between the time of Jesus, the last Prophet of the Children of Israel, and Muhammad, the final Prophet. During this gap, no Prophet was sent, and the world fell into widespread idolatry and false worship. Muhammad was sent to guide people back to the truth when the earth was filled with evil, and the true teachings of earlier Prophets had been largely forgotten. In his final sermon, the Prophet described how most of the earth’s people had deviated from the true path, with only a few remaining faithful to the teachings of previous Prophets. Allah had chosen this moment to send Muhammad with an eternal book, ensuring that people would no longer have an excuse of not receiving a messenger. Allah is fully capable of rewarding the obedient and punishing the disobedient.
