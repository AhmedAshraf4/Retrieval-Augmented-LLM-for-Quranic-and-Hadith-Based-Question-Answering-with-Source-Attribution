Allah commands the believers to show enmity to the idolators and dissociate from them, stating that they do not deserve a covenant of peace due to their shirk (polytheism) and disbelief in the Prophet Muhammad. If these idolators gain the upper hand, they will bring corruption, break ties of kinship, and violate their vows. The term "ill" refers to kinship, while "dhimmah" refers to a covenant.
