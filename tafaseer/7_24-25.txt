Allah commanded Adam, Hawwa', Iblis, and possibly the snake to descend to earth. The enmity is primarily between Adam and Iblis, with Hawwa' following Adam. The snake, if mentioned, is considered a follower of Iblis. The earth is designated as a dwelling and place of enjoyment for them, with fixed terms decreed by Allah's predestination. They will live, die, and be resurrected from the earth, as stated by Allah. This fulfills the promise of the earth being their final abode for this life, where they will experience death and resurrection for judgment on the Day of Resurrection, when all beings will be gathered and rewarded or punished based on their deeds.
