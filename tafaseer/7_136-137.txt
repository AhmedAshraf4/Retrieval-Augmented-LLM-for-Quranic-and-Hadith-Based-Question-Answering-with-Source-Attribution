When the people of Firawn rebelled and persisted in their disobedience, despite being shown many consecutive signs, Allah drowned them in the sea. Musa, by Allah's power, parted the sea for him and the Children of Israel, allowing them to pass through safely. When Firawn and his army followed them into the sea, the waters closed in on them, and they drowned because they rejected Allah's signs. Allah then granted the Children of Israel, who had been oppressed, the inheritance of the eastern and western lands, referring to the blessed region of the Sham area. This fulfilled the promise of Allah to elevate the oppressed and make them rulers, as seen in His plan to make them inheritors and establish them in the land. Allah also destroyed what Fir`awn and his people had built, including their agriculture and structures.
