Those who reject the signs of Allah questioned the Prophet Muhammad’s sanity, claiming he was mad. However, the Prophet was not mad; he was a true messenger of Allah, sent to warn people clearly of the impending punishment. Allah instructed them to reflect and observe the Prophet’s actions: when he called the Quraysh on Mount As-Safa, urging them to heed Allah’s warning, some mocked him, saying he was mad for shouting all night. In response, Allah revealed that Muhammad was not insane but a plain warner, urging them to recognize the truth through reflection and sincerity, standing for Allah individually or in groups, without bias.
