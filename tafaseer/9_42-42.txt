Tafsir Explanation: Allah rebukes those who failed to join the Prophet in the battle of Tabuk, pretending to have valid reasons for staying behind. If the journey had been short or the reward immediate, they would have followed the Prophet. However, because the journey to Ash-Sham was long, they made excuses. When the Prophet returned, they would swear by Allah that if they had been able, they would have joined the expedition. In reality, they were deceiving themselves, and Allah knew they were lying.
