Tafsir Explanation: Some idolators told Muslims to abandon the religion of Muhammad and follow them. In response, Allah revealed a verse, comparing those who revert to disbelief after receiving guidance to a man who loses his way while traveling with companions. The devils lead him astray, and despite his companions calling him to return to the correct path, he refuses. This is the fate of those who, after recognizing the truth of Islam, follow false gods. Allah’s guidance is the only true guidance, and those who submit to Him and worship Him alone, performing prayer and fearing Him, will be gathered before Him on the Day of Resurrection. Allah is the Creator of the heavens and the earth, and He controls all affairs. On the Day of Judgment, when the Trumpet is blown, the dominion will belong to Allah alone, and all will be subject to His command.
