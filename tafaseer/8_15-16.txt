Tafsir Explanation: Fleeing from the battlefield is prohibited, and the punishment for it is severe. Allah warns believers not to turn their backs to the disbelievers when meeting them in battle, as doing so, unless it is a tactical maneuver or retreat to a troop for support, is forbidden. A retreat for regrouping, seeking assistance from another group, or gathering intelligence on the enemy is allowed. However, fleeing for any other reason is a major sin, as it leads to Allah’s wrath and Hell as a final destination. The Prophet Muhammad described fleeing the battlefield as one of the seven destructive sins, alongside acts like murder, magic, and consuming interest.
