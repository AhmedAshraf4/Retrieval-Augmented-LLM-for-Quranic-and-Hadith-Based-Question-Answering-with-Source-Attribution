Firawn gathered magicians to confront Musa, promising them great rewards and provisions if they succeeded in defeating him. He offered them status, aiming to make them part of his inner circle. When they trusted Firawn’s promises, they prepared to perform their magic. The magicians threw their ropes and sticks, which transformed into snakes, to challenge Musa. This action directly fulfilled their expectation of being rewarded and honored by Fir`awn if they succeeded.
