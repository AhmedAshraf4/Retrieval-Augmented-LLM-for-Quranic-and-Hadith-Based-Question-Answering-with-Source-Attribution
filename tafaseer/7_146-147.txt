Tafsir Explanation: Allah declares that He will turn away from His signs those who are arrogantly proud on earth without justification, depriving their hearts of understanding. These individuals, too proud to obey Allah, are left in ignorance, as their hearts and eyes are turned away from guidance due to their rejection of Allah’s signs. Even if they witness all the miracles, they will not believe, nor will they choose the righteous path when it is shown to them. Instead, they persist in misguidance, because they have denied Allah’s signs and were heedless of them. Consequently, their actions are rendered worthless, and they will be recompensed only for what they have done, receiving good for good and evil for evil.
