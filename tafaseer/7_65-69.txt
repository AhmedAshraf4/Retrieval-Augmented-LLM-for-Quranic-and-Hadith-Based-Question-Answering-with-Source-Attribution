Tafsir Explanation: Hud, a prophet sent by Allah, was chosen from the people of Ad, descendants of Ad, son of Iram, and lived in Yemen in the land of Ahqaf, known for its sand mounds. The people of `Ad were mighty and strong, with large statues marking their land, but they were arrogant and denied the truth. Hud called them to worship Allah alone and warned them against idol worship. They ridiculed him, accusing him of foolishness and lying. Hud defended himself, declaring he was a messenger of the Lord of all worlds, bringing the truth and acting as a sincere advisor. He reminded them of Allah's favor, mentioning how they were made successors of the people of Noah and granted physical strength. Hud urged them to remember Allah’s blessings and turn to Him for success.
