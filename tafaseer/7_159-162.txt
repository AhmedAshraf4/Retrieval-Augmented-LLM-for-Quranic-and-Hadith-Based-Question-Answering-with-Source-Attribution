Tafsir Explanation: A group of People of the Scripture humbly submit to Allah, believing in both the Qur'an and the scriptures revealed to them. They recite the verses of Allah at night, prostrating in prayer, and do not trade Allah's revelations for trivial gains. When the Qur'an is recited to those who received the previous scriptures, they acknowledge it as the truth from their Lord and recognize their submission to Allah even before its revelation. They fall in prostration, weeping in humility, and express their belief in the fulfillment of Allah's promises. These individuals will receive double the reward for their patience and steadfastness.
