Allah granted the believers a great favor by sending Muhammad as a Messenger from among themselves, reciting His verses, purifying them from sins, and teaching them the Qur'an and wisdom. This guidance removed them from the darkness of disbelief to the light of faith, elevated their knowledge, hearts, and speech, and distinguished them as devout and learned among people. Allah commands the believers to remember Him and be grateful, promising that if they mention Him, He will mention them in return, drawing closer to those who draw near to Him. Those who deny this favor replace Allah’s blessings with disbelief, leading their people to ruin. Gratitude toward Allah through remembrance and faith brings increase in blessings, while ingratitude leads to severe punishment. Allah also favors seeing the effect of His bounties manifest in His servants.
