Tafsir Explanation: Men are appointed as the protectors and maintainers of women, responsible for their care, leadership, and discipline when necessary, due to their greater responsibility in certain matters such as prophethood and leadership positions. Men are also required to provide for women financially, including dowries and other expenses. A righteous woman is obedient to her husband, guards her honor and property in his absence, and fulfills her duties, earning reward such as entry into Paradise. If a woman displays ill conduct, such as disobedience or disrespect, her husband should first advise her. If that does not resolve the issue, he may refrain from sharing her bed or speaking to her. If these steps still fail, a light, non-harmful form of discipline is allowed. However, if the woman returns to obedience, the husband must not seek further means of annoyance. Allah warns men that He will avenge any unjust treatment toward their wives.
