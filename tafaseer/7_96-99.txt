Tafsir Explanation: The people of the towns that were sent Messengers had little faith, unlike the people of Prophet Yunus, who believed after facing punishment, and their belief spared them from further torment. Allah explains that if the people of the towns had believed and shown reverence for Him, He would have blessed them with rain and vegetation from the heavens and the earth. However, they denied the Messengers, so they were punished for their wickedness. Allah warns that the people who live heedlessly, believing they are safe from His punishment, will be caught off guard by His plan, whether at night while they sleep or during the day while they play. None can feel secure from Allah’s punishment except those who are lost in their delusions.
