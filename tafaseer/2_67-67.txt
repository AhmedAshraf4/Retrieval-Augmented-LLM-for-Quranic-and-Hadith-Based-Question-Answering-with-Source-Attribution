A man from the Children of Israel was killed, and his nephew murdered him to gain his inheritance, then secretly placed the body at another man’s door and demanded revenge, causing the people to nearly fight. They went to Moses, who conveyed God’s command to slaughter a cow, but they mocked him and disputed, making the command increasingly specific instead of slaughtering any cow. They eventually found the exact cow owned by a single man and paid its full skin in gold to obtain it. After slaughtering the cow, they struck the dead man with part of it, and God brought him back to life. He identified his nephew as the murderer, then died again. The murderer was exposed and prevented from inheriting, and the killing was solved through the cow as commanded.
