Allah commands Prophet Muhammad to call on the disbelievers to cease their rebellion and disbelief, urging them to embrace Islam and repentance, assuring them that their past sins will be forgiven. If they persist in their disobedience, they will face the same punishment as previous nations who were destroyed for their defiance. The believers are instructed to fight against them until there is no more fitnah (persecution) and all worship is for Allah alone. The purpose of fighting is to establish Tawhid, the sincere worship of Allah without any partners. If the disbelievers cease their rebellion and accept Islam, their blood and wealth will be protected, and they will be treated as fellow believers. However, if they continue to defy, Allah remains the protector and supporter of the believers, providing them with strength and victory against their enemies.
