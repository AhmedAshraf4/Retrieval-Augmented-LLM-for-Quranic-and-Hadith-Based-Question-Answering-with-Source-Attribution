Tafsir Explanation: Musa, after his anger subsided, took up the Tablets he had thrown in anger due to his people's worship of the calf. The Tablets, which had been shattered when thrown, contained guidance and mercy for those who fear their Lord. Although the specifics of the law were lost, Musa found in the inscriptions guidance. The shattered pieces were believed to have remained in the safes of some Israelite kings until the rise of the Islamic State, but Allah alone knows the truth of this claim.
