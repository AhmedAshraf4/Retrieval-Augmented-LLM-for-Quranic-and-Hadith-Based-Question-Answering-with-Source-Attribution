Tafsir Explanation: Prophet Shuayb warned his people against blocking the roads and threatening travelers with death for their money, likening them to bandits. He condemned their actions of hindering the believers who came to him and trying to distort the path of Allah. He reminded them of their past weakness when they were few, and how Allah granted them strength by increasing their numbers. Shuayb urged them to reflect on the fate of previous corrupt nations who faced punishment for disobeying Allah and rejecting His messengers. He acknowledged the division within his people, with some believing in his message and others not, and instructed those who believed to be patient until Allah judged between them, for He is the best of judges.
