Allah warns the hypocrites, who oppose the believers by promoting evil, forbidding good, and withholding from spending in His cause. They have neglected the remembrance of Allah, leading to their abandonment by Him in return. Allah will treat them as if He has forgotten them, and it will be said to them on the Day of Judgment, "This Day We will forget you as you forgot the meeting of this Day of yours." The hypocrites, along with disbelievers, are promised the eternal punishment of Hell for their rebellious actions. Allah has cursed them, expelling them from His mercy, and they will endure a lasting torment.
