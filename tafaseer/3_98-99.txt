Allah criticizes the disbelieving People of the Book for rejecting the truth and blocking others from the path of Allah, despite knowing that the message brought by the Prophet is the truth. They were aware from previous Prophets that a final Prophet would arise, an unlettered Arab from Makkah, the master of the Children of Adam. They were even informed about his coming and commanded to announce it, but they denied him. Allah warns them that He is fully aware of their actions and will hold them accountable for their defiance.
