The verse highlights the severing of ties between Muslims and disbelievers. It emphasizes that the disbelievers are supporters of each other, while Muslims are not to offer support to them. The Prophet stated that a Muslim does not inherit from a disbeliever, nor does a disbeliever inherit from a Muslim. The verse warns that if Muslims fail to protect and support each other, it will lead to widespread fitnah (trial) and corruption on the earth, where polytheism and mischief would spread, causing confusion and division among people.
