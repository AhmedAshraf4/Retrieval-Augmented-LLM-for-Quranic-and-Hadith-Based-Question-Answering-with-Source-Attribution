Allah has left no excuse for the idolators by sending them the Book and the Messenger, which are explained in detail with perfect knowledge. The idolators are reminded that they will face no punishment until a Messenger has been sent to warn them. On the Day of Resurrection, those who neglected the truth in this life will regret their actions. They will acknowledge that the Messengers brought the truth and will ask if there are any intercessors to save them from their fate, or if they can be sent back to live differently. However, even if they were returned to life, they would revert to their previous sinful actions. Their false deities will fail to intercede for them, and they will realize that they have lost themselves and that their fabricated beliefs will not save them.
