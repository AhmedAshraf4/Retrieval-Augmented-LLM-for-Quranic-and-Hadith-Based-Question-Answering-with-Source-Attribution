The Prophet does not know the unseen, nor can he bring benefit or harm to himself. Allah instructed the Prophet to place all matters in His hands and to clarify that his knowledge of the unseen is limited to what Allah reveals to him. If the Prophet knew the unseen, he would have accumulated wealth and avoided harm, such as poverty or misfortune. Allah's statement that He alone is the Knower of the unseen highlights this distinction. The Prophet's role is to warn people of the consequences of their actions and to bring good news of Paradise for the believers. Allah made the Qur'an easy for him to speak, so he could deliver glad tidings to the righteous and warn the most contentious people.
