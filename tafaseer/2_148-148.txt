Every nation has its own Qiblah for prayer. Jews face their direction, Christians face theirs, and Muslims are guided to the true Qiblah appointed by Allah. Allah can gather all people wherever they are, even if their bodies have disintegrated, and He has power over all things. This demonstrates that while each nation may follow its own prescribed direction in worship, the believers follow the Qiblah set by Allah, and ultimately all will be brought together by Him.
