They are those who believe in the unseen, establish the prayer, spend from what God has granted them, believe in what was revealed to the Messenger and to the messengers before him, and hold certain belief in the Hereafter. They prepare for the Hereafter by performing righteous deeds and avoiding prohibited acts. As a result, they are upon guidance, meaning they follow a clear light and possess insight granted by God. Because of this guidance, they are the successful ones in this world and the Hereafter. They attain what they seek, are protected from the evil they feared, receive reward and eternal life in Paradise, and are kept safe from the punishment prepared for God’s enemies.
