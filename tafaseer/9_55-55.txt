Allah warns not to be amazed by the wealth or children of disbelievers, as these are worldly tests. In other verses, Allah clarifies that the enjoyment of worldly life is a trial for them, meant to test their faith. Their wealth and children are not signs of divine favor but tools of punishment, leading to their gradual downfall. Allah's plan is for them to suffer in this life, with their wealth and children being part of their test, eventually leading to their death while remaining disbelievers, making their punishment more severe. This is exemplified by the obligation to pay Zakah, which is intended to purify their wealth and redirect it towards Allah’s cause, emphasizing the futility of relying on worldly gains for salvation.
