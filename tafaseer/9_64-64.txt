The hypocrites feared their secrets being exposed. They would say to each other that they hoped Allah would not reveal their hidden matters. This is similar to another verse where the hypocrites question why Allah does not punish them for their words, while in reality, Hell will be their fate. Allah responded to their fear, stating that He will certainly expose all they dread, revealing their true nature to the Messenger through revelation. Those with hypocrisy in their hearts will have their ill-wills uncovered, and their speech will betray them. This Surah, known as "Al-Fadihah" (the Exposing), uncovers the hypocrites and their concealed deceit.
