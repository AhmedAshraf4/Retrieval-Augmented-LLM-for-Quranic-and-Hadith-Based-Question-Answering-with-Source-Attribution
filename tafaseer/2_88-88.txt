They said that their hearts were wrapped, meaning screened, covered, and stamped so they could not understand or accept what was brought to them, and they claimed that their hearts already contained all knowledge and had no need for what Muhammad brought. This claim was false. Rather, God expelled them and deprived them of righteousness because of their disbelief, placing a curse and a seal upon their hearts. As a result, only a small number of them believed, or their belief was so minimal that it had no benefit, since it was overwhelmed by their rejection of what was revealed to Muhammad.
