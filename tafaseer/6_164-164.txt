Tafsir Explanation: Allah commands Muhammad to declare that He alone is the Lord of all things, and thus, it is only to Him that one should turn for trust and protection. This trust is emphasized as an essential aspect of sincere worship, which is central to faith. No person will bear the sins of another, and each will be judged according to their own deeds. On the Day of Resurrection, everyone will be recompensed justly, with no injustice or reduction in reward. Even though people cannot bear the burdens of others, Allah will elevate the righteous believers' offspring to share in their reward due to their shared faith, while the righteous will be rewarded individually for their own actions. Ultimately, all will return to Allah, where He will resolve all disputes and clarify the truth of each person’s deeds.
