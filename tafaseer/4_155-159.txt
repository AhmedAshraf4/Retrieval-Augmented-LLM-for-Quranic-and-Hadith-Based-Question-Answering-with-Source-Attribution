The Jews were cursed for their repeated sins, breaking their promises to Allah and rejecting His signs, including killing many of His Prophets. They claimed their hearts were sealed, but Allah stated their disbelief had sealed their hearts, leading to little faith. They falsely accused Maryam of fornication and claimed that they killed Isa. Allah clarified that they neither killed nor crucified him but that it appeared so to them. In reality, Allah raised Isa to Himself. The Jews, in their hatred and jealousy of Isa's miracles, sought to harm him, but when they conspired with the king of Damascus to arrest him, Allah made Isa ascend to the heavens while a young man was made to look like him and crucified in his place. This event confused the Jews and some Christians who mistakenly accepted the false claim. Allah decreed that Isa would return before the Day of Judgment, where all People of the Scripture will believe in him before his death. Isa will descend, defeat the false messiah (Ad-Dajjal), and rule with justice, breaking the cross, killing the pig, and abolishing the Jizyah. During his time, there will be peace, and wealth will be abundant. After his death, a wind will take the souls of all believers, and only the evil ones will remain, leading to the onset of the Hour. `Isa will bear witness against those who rejected him on the Day of Resurrection, confirming that he was a servant and messenger of Allah.
