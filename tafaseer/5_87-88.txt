Tafsir Explanation: Some Companions of the Prophet proposed adopting a life of celibacy and renouncing worldly desires, similar to the practices of monks. When the Prophet heard of this, he clarified that his Sunnah involves fasting and breaking the fast, praying, sleeping, and marrying women, and whoever follows his Sunnah is of him. He also rebuked those who sought to abandon permissible acts, stating that he neither forbids lawful things nor requires extremes in worship. Allah's command is clear: do not make unlawful what Allah has made lawful, and avoid excessive indulgence or denial. Instead, maintain a balanced approach in all aspects of life, including eating, drinking, and spending, seeking Allah’s pleasure through moderation and righteousness.
