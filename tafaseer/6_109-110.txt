The idolators swore strong oaths that if they were given a miracle, they would believe in it. Allah responded, saying that signs are only from Him, and He grants them at His will. The idolators’ vows were not sincere; even if a sign came, they would not believe. Allah explains that their hearts and eyes will be turned away from guidance, just as they rejected belief the first time. Even if they were sent back to life, they would revert to their previous disbelief. Allah leaves them to wander blindly in their rebellion and misguidance, as they continue in their defiance and refusal to believe.
