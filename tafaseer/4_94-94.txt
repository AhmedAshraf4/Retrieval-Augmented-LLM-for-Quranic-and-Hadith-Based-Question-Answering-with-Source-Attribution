Tafsir Explanation: A man from Bani Sulaym, tending sheep, greeted the Companions of the Prophet with peace. They suspected his greeting was a tactic to protect himself, so they killed him and took his sheep. This act led to the revelation of the verse, condemning the unjust killing of someone who greeted in peace, showing faith, and emphasizing the dangers of seeking worldly gains at the cost of righteousness. The verse warns against labeling someone who greets in peace as a non-believer, highlighting the significance of verifying situations, especially in conflict, and reminding that Allah's rewards far exceed worldly profits. The verse also draws attention to how the Muslims were once in a similar position, hiding their faith, until Allah bestowed His blessings upon them. It serves as a reminder to act with caution, as Allah is fully aware of all actions.
