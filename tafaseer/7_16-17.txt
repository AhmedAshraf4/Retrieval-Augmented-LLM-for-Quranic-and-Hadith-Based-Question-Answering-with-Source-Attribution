Tafsir Explanation: Iblis, after being expelled, vowed to mislead humanity by waiting for them on the straight path. He declared that, as Allah had caused his ruin, he would obstruct humans from worshiping Allah by leading them astray from the truth. He aimed to influence them from every direction: from before, behind, right, and left, seeking to create doubts about the Hereafter, entice them toward worldly desires, confuse their faith, and lure them into sin. Iblis' actions were part of a divine test, distinguishing the believers in the Hereafter from those in doubt. Despite Iblis' efforts, only a small group of true believers followed him, fulfilling his assumption. This highlights the challenge of staying on the straight path and the constant danger of Iblis’ deception, encouraging the seeking of refuge from all his traps.
