Tafsir Explanation: On the Day of Resurrection, Allah will question the Messengers about how their nations responded to their teachings. The Messengers will reply, "We have no knowledge," acknowledging their lack of insight into the hidden intentions of their people, for only Allah has complete knowledge of all things, including what is hidden in people's hearts. This response highlights their respect for Allah's supreme knowledge and emphasizes that their understanding was limited to visible actions, not internal thoughts. The Messengers’ submission to Allah's omniscience reflects their humility before Him.
