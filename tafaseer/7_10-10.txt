Allah reminds His servants of His blessings, highlighting how He made the earth a stable home, with mountains to anchor it and rivers for sustenance. He created the clouds to bring rain, enabling them to grow crops, and provided various means for earning, including trade and professions. Despite these abundant benefits, most people remain ungrateful. Allah notes that even if one tried to count His blessings, they would never be able to enumerate them, as humans are often unjust and ungrateful.
