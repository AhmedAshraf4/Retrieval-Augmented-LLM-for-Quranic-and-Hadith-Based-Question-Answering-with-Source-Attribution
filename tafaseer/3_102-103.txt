Tafsir Explanation: Allah commands to have Taqwa of Him as is His due, meaning He should be obeyed, remembered, and appreciated. This Taqwa is linked to preserving one's Islam and remaining steadfast in it until death. Allah also instructs to hold fast to the Rope of Allah, which refers to His covenant, and warns against division within the Muslim community. The Aws and Khazraj tribes, once enemies during the time of Jahiliyyah, became brothers through Allah's grace when they embraced Islam, overcoming their past hatred and enmity. The Messenger of Allah reminded them of this favor, highlighting that it was through Allah's intervention that their hearts were united. The ayah emphasizes that true unity and brotherhood among believers is a gift from Allah and not achievable through worldly means alone.
