Allah commands the believers to firmly maintain their faith in Him, His Messenger, the Qur'an, and the previously revealed scriptures. This is not a redundant call but a directive to continuously uphold and strengthen one's belief. The believer asks in every prayer for guidance to remain on the straight path, seeking Allah's help to increase and preserve their guidance. Disbelief in Allah, His angels, His books, His messengers, and the Last Day leads to great deviation from the correct path. Those who repeatedly turn from belief to disbelief, increasing in their rejection, will not receive Allah’s forgiveness nor guidance, and will face severe punishment.
