Tafsir Explanation: On the Day of Judgment, Allah will gather the Jinn and the humans who followed them, those who sought refuge with the Jinn, obeyed them, and were misled by their deceitful influence. Allah will address the Jinn, pointing out how they led many humans astray. He will remind them that He had commanded mankind not to worship Satan, who is their clear enemy, but instead to worship Him alone, the straight path. The humans who were misled will acknowledge how they benefited from the Jinn, as they used to invoke them for protection and assistance, especially during the time of ignorance. In response, the Jinn will claim responsibility for leading both themselves and the humans astray. Allah will then decree that the Fire be their eternal dwelling, where they will remain forever unless He wills otherwise.
