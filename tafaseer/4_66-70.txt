Tafsīr Explanation: Allah acknowledges that if the people had been commanded to kill themselves or their guilty ones, most would still have rejected the command, showing their rebellious nature. However, if they obeyed His commands and refrained from prohibitions, it would have been better for them, strengthening their faith and earning them great rewards from Allah, including guidance to the straight path. Those who obey Allah and His Messenger will be honored by being placed with the Prophets, the truthful, the martyrs, and the righteous, forming a blessed company. This companionship is a great reward, as exemplified by the Prophet's choice to be with them in the Hereafter. The companionship is based not on deeds alone, but on Allah's mercy, and those who love the Prophets and righteous will be resurrected with them, as Allah's favor is granted by His wisdom and knowledge of who truly deserves His guidance.
