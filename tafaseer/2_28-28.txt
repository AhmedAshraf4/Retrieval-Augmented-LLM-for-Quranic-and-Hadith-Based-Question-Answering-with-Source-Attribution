How can people deny Allah while He brought them from non-existence into life. They did not exist at all, then He created them and gave them life. After this life, He will cause them to die, and then He will give them life again at the Resurrection. This sequence establishes Allah alone as the Creator, Sustainer, and absolute authority over His servants. Denial is irrational because creation, life, death, and return to life all occur by His action alone, not by themselves and not by any other power.
