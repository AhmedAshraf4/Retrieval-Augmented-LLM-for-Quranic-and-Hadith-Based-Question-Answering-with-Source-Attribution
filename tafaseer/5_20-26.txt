Tafsir Explanation: Musa reminded the Children of Israel of Allah's numerous favors, including sending Prophets among them, granting them kingship, and providing them with blessings no other nation received. Despite this, when Musa ordered them to fight and enter Jerusalem, they refused due to the strength of the inhabitants. Two righteous men, Yuwsha' and Kalib, urged them to trust in Allah and obey His command, promising victory if they did. However, the people insisted on waiting for the enemy to leave, and their disobedience led to Allah's punishment, condemning them to wander for forty years. During this time, many miracles occurred, and after the forty years, Yuwsha' led them to conquer Jerusalem. Despite Allah's command to enter the city humbly, they disrespected His order and entered arrogantly. Allah then reminded Musa not to grieve over the rebellious people, as they had earned their fate through their refusal to obey His command and engage in Jihad.
