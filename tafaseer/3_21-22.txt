Tafsir Explanation: The People of the Book are reprimanded for their rejection of Allah's signs and Messengers, both in the past and more recently. Their defiance was evident in their denial of the truth and refusal to follow it. They killed many Prophets who merely called them to the truth without any wrongdoing on their part. They also killed those who called for justice and fairness. Their arrogance, marked by rejecting the truth and belittling others, led to their punishment. As a result, Allah humiliated them in this life and promised them painful torment in the Hereafter. Their actions will be rendered worthless both in this world and the next, and they will have no helpers.
