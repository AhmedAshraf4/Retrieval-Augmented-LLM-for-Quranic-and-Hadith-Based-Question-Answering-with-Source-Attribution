When a man divorces his wife with a reversible divorce and her Iddah (waiting period) nears its end, he is instructed to either reconcile with her in a good manner, ensuring witnesses, or release her kindly without dispute, harshness, or bad words. This guidance forbids taking a wife back to harm her, such as delaying her Iddah to prevent her from remarrying, which was a practice that some men used. Allah prohibits this behavior, warning that those who do so wrong themselves. The verse also cautions against treating Allah’s laws lightly or as a joke. The message emphasizes remembering Allah’s favors, including His guidance through the Qur'an and Sunnah, and fearing Him in all matters, as He is fully aware of everything.
