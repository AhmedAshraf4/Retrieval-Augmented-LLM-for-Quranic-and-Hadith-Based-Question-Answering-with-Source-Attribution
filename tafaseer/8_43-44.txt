Tafsir Explanation: Allah made the enemy appear few in the eyes of the believers and vice versa to encourage them before the Battle of Badr. In a dream, the Prophet saw the enemy as a small number, which strengthened the resolve of the believers. If the disbelievers had been shown as many, the believers might have hesitated and fought amongst themselves. Allah's intervention led the believers to see the enemy as a smaller force, making them eager to confront them. When one of the disbelievers was captured, he revealed their true number was a thousand. This vision was part of Allah's plan to ensure the battle unfolded as ordained, with the believers feeling confident and the disbelievers seeing the believers as more numerous. Allah's support came with a thousand angels, and the disbelievers saw the believers as twice their number. This event served as a sign of Allah’s favor and wisdom.
