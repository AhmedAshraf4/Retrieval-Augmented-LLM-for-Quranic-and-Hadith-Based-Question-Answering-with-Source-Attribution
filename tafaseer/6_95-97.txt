Allah causes the seed grain and fruit stone to split and sprout, bringing forth living plants from lifeless objects. He also brings the living from the dead and the dead from the living, as seen in His creation of plants from seeds, and the wicked offspring from righteous parents. Allah is the One who controls all such transformations. He is the Creator of light and darkness, bringing daybreak and night, with each having its purpose, such as providing rest during the night. Allah also sets the sun and moon in their fixed orbits, ensuring precise reckoning and the alternation of day and night. The sun and moon follow their courses exactly, according to the decree of the All-Knowing. Allah has also made the stars for guidance in the darkness of land and sea. All these events, from the creation of the heavens and earth to the regulation of celestial bodies, demonstrate Allah's absolute power and knowledge, which is fully explained for those who understand.
