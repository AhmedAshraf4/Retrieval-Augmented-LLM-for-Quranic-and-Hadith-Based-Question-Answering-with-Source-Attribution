The angels delivered the good news to Maryam that she would give birth to a son, named Isa, who would come into existence by Allah’s command, with the word "Be!" His name, Al-Masih, reflects his ability to heal the sick by Allah’s permission. Isa would be honored in this world and the Hereafter, being a leader and interceding for others by Allah’s will. He would speak as an infant in the cradle and as an adult, calling people to worship Allah alone. Maryam, astonished, asked how she could have a son without a man’s touch, and the angel explained that Allah creates whatever He wills, and with His command, things exist instantly, just as He wills.
