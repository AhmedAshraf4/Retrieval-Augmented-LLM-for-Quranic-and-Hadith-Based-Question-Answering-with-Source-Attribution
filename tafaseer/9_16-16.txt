Tafsir Explanation: The wisdom behind Jihad is to test the Muslims, distinguishing those with sincere intent from those with false intent. Allah does not leave the believers untested; He tests those who strive in His cause and remain loyal to Him, His Messenger, and the believers, both outwardly and inwardly. Allah has stated that this test serves to reveal the true believers and expose the liars, as those before were tested similarly. It is not the case that believers will enter Paradise without being tested, for Allah will distinguish the patient and those who fought for His cause. The test serves to separate the good from the wicked, fulfilling the purpose of distinguishing between obedience and disobedience. Allah is fully aware of all events—past, present, and future—and no one can alter His will or judgment.
