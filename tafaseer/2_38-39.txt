Allah sent Adam, Eve, and Satan down to the earth from Paradise and informed them that guidance would come to their offspring through revealed Books and sent Prophets and Messengers with clear signs and plain explanation. Whoever follows this guidance by accepting what is in the revealed Books and what the Messengers bring will have no fear regarding the Hereafter and no grief regarding worldly life, and will not go astray in this world nor become miserable in the next. Those who turn away from this guidance and reject or deny the signs are described as disbelievers who will face hardship and loss, and they will be raised for punishment and remain in the Fire permanently with no escape.
