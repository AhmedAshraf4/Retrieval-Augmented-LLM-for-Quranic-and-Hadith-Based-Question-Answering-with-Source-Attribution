Tafsir Explanation: A man from the Ansar initially embraced Islam but later reverted to polytheism. Feeling regretful, he sent a message asking if he could repent. Allah then revealed that those who disbelieve after believing, having witnessed the truth of the Messenger and clear proofs, do not deserve guidance, as they willingly chose disbelief over belief. Allah curses such people, and they will remain in His curse and face unrelenting punishment. However, Allah also declared that those who repent and perform righteous deeds after such rejection will find forgiveness, as He is Most Merciful and Oft-Forgiving. This illustrates Allah's mercy towards those who sincerely return to Him.
