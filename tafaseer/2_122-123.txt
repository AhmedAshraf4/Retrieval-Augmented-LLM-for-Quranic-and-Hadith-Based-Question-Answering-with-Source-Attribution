Allah reminded the People of the Scriptures of the Ummi Prophet and Messenger, described in their Books by his name, characteristics, and the good news about him and his Ummah. They were warned not to conceal this information, which was a favor from Allah. They were commanded to remember their daily life and religious obligations and the blessings Allah had given them. They should not envy the Arabs for receiving the Final Messenger, who was an Arab, and envy should not lead them to oppose, deny, or refuse to follow him until the Day of Judgment.
