Tafsīr Explanation: The people of the Fire will call out to the people of Paradise, asking for water or food, but they will be denied. Allah has forbidden such favors to the disbelievers, and the believers will respond that Allah has prohibited them due to their disbelief. The disbelievers are described as those who treated religion as a game and were deceived by worldly pleasures, neglecting the Hereafter. As a result, on the Day of Judgment, Allah will forget them as they forgot the meeting of that Day, meaning they will be forsaken and excluded from mercy, just as they neglected the signs of Allah in their lives. This forsaking is a just recompense for their disregard. Allah will ask the servant in the Hereafter if He had not blessed them with favors in their worldly life, to which the servant will acknowledge. Allah will then remind them that they failed to prepare for the meeting with Him, and thus He will forget them as they had forgotten Him.
