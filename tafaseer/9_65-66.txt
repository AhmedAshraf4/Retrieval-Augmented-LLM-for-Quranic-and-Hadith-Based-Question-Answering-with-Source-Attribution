Tafsir Explanation: During the Battle of Tabuk, a man criticized the Prophet’s companions, calling them cowardly, gluttonous, and dishonest. A witness reported this to the Prophet, and part of the Qur'an was revealed in response. The man later sought to justify his words by claiming he had been joking, holding onto the Prophet’s camel as stones fell on him, but the Prophet recited the verse condemning their mockery of Allah, His signs, and His Messenger. Allah declared that their excuse was unacceptable, as they had disbelieved after having once believed. While some might be pardoned, others would be punished for their criminal act of mockery.
