Tafsir Explanation: Allah relates the stories of various towns to Prophet Muhammad, where their Messengers came with clear signs and proofs, yet the people did not believe. Despite recognizing the truth, they rejected it, and as a result, they were not guided when the truth came to them again. Allah seals the hearts of the disbelievers, preventing them from accepting the guidance they previously denied. The people, instead of honoring their original covenant with Allah, which was instilled in them through their Fitrah, turned to rebellion and worshipped others. This deviation from the natural monotheistic belief is further illustrated by the Hadith which says Allah created humans to be monotheists, but the devils led them astray, altering their beliefs. The majority of the past nations broke their covenant with Allah, rejecting the divine guidance brought by His Messengers.
