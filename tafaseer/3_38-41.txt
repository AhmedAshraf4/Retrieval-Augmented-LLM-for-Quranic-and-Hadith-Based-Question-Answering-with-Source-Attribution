Zakariyya, seeing Allah provide Maryam with fruits from different seasons, desired a child. Despite his old age, weakness, and his wife's infertility, he prayed earnestly to Allah for a righteous offspring. While standing in prayer, the angels announced to him the good news of a son, Yahya, who would believe in the Word from Allah, be noble, wise, and pious, immune from illicit relationships, and a prophet from the righteous. Zakariyya, surprised by the news given his age and his wife's barrenness, asked how this could happen. The angel responded that Allah does as He wills. Zakariyya then requested a sign, and Allah told him that he would be unable to speak for three days except through signals. During this time, he was instructed to remember and glorify Allah.
