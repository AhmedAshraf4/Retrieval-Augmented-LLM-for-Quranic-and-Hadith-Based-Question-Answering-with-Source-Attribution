Tafsir Explanation: Idolators will enjoy their portion in this world, which is determined by their deeds, provisions, and lifespan. However, when death approaches, the angels come to take their souls, frightening them. They will be asked where their false gods are, those whom they used to invoke instead of Allah. The idolators will admit that these partners have vanished and can no longer aid them, confessing their disbelief. They will bear witness against themselves, acknowledging their rejection of faith.
