The Children of Israel killed a man and disputed over who committed the crime, with each group accusing the other, while hiding the truth. God revealed what they concealed and commanded them to strike the dead man with a part of the cow. They struck him with it, and he came back to life, directly identifying his killer, which ended their dispute. This act showed that any part of the cow was sufficient for the command and that specifying it was unnecessary. By restoring the murdered man to life after death, God demonstrated His power to give life to the dead and made this event a clear proof against them that resurrection is real and unavoidable.
