Tafsir Explanation: The "manifest serpent" refers to the male snake that Musa threw his staff to transform into, which grew large and rushed towards Pharaoh. As it approached, Pharaoh, terrified, jumped from his throne and called out to Musa to remove the snake, promising to believe in him and release the Children of Israel. Musa then made the snake return to a staff. In the second sign, Musa drew his hand from his cloak, and it shone brightly, not due to illness or leprosy, as clarified by Ibn Abbas. After placing his hand back in his cloak, it returned to its normal color.
