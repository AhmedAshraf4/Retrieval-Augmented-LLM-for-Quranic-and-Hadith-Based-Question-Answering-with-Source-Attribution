Allah affirms the Prophet Muhammad’s prophethood and His revealed Qur'an, testifying that it is free from falsehood, whether from before or after. The Qur'an is sent down with Allah's knowledge, covering guidance, truth, and the unseen. No one can comprehend Allah's knowledge except what He wills. The angels also bear witness to the truth of what Muhammad brought, and Allah alone suffices as a witness. Those who disbelieve and hinder others from the path of Allah have strayed far, and Allah will not guide them except to Hell, where they will remain forever. Allah urges people to believe in the truth brought by His Messenger, for it is better for them. If they reject, Allah owns all in the heavens and earth, needing nothing from their faith. Allah is All-Knowing and All-Wise in guiding or leading astray those who deserve it.
