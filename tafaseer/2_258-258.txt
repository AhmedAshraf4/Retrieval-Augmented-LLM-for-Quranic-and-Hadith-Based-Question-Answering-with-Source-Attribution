King Nimrod, who ruled with tyranny and arrogance, disputed with Ibrahim about the existence of Allah. He claimed to be the giver of life and death, but Ibrahim countered by asserting that his Lord is the one who gives life and causes death, evidenced by the creation and destruction around them. To challenge Nimrod's claim, Ibrahim asked him to bring the sun from the west if he truly had control over life and death, as Allah makes the sun rise from the east each day. Unable to respond, Nimrod was left silent, proving his falsehood. This event demonstrated that those who reject the truth are deprived of valid arguments and will face Allah’s anger and punishment.
