This ayah condemns those who claim to love Allah but do not follow the way of Muhammad. True love for Allah is demonstrated by following the Messenger's teachings in every action, word, and condition. Allah commands that if people truly love Him, they must follow Muhammad, and in doing so, Allah will love them and forgive their sins. This submission to Muhammad's way is essential, and any defiance of it is considered disbelieving, regardless of any claims of loving Allah. Allah does not accept such disobedience, emphasizing that even the previous Prophets, had they been alive during Muhammad’s time, would have followed him and his law.
