Tafsir Explanation: The disciples of Jesus, son of Mary, asked him to pray to Allah for a table of food to be sent down from heaven. They requested it to strengthen their faith, confirm Jesus' truthfulness, and witness a divine sign. Jesus prayed to Allah for this sign to be a festival for them and future generations. Allah granted their request, sending the table with seven fish and seven pieces of bread, which they ate as much as they desired. However, when some disciples began to steal from the table, fearing it would not return, the table ascended. Allah warned that if anyone disbelieved after receiving this sign, they would face a punishment worse than anyone else, emphasizing the severity of rejecting His signs.
