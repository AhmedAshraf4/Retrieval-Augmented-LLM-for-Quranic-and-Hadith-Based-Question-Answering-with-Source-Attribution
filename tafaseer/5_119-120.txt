Tafsir Explanation: On the Day of Resurrection, only truth will benefit those who were truthful. `Isa, son of Maryam, disowns the disbelieving Christians who falsely attributed lies to Allah and His Messenger. He refers their fate to the will of Allah. On this day, those who believed in the oneness of Allah will be rewarded, entering Paradise where rivers flow beneath gardens, and they will dwell there forever, never to be removed. Allah will be pleased with them, and they with Him. The greatest reward is Allah's pleasure. This ultimate success is unparalleled. Allah is the sovereign of the heavens and the earth, and everything within them is under His control and will, as He is capable of all things.
