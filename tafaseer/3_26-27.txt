Tafsir Explanation: Allah holds all sovereignty, granting and removing power, honor, and humiliation as He wills. He transferred prophethood from the Children of Israel to the unlettered Prophet Muhammad, the final Messenger to all mankind and jinn. Allah endowed Muhammad with unique qualities, surpassing those of previous prophets, including knowledge of past and future events, and granted his Ummah dominance across the world. Allah's will dictates who receives His mercy, and no one can challenge His decision, as He knows best where to place His message. Allah controls the alternation of day and night, adjusting their lengths across seasons, and brings life from the dead, such as from seeds to plants or faithful to disbelievers. He provides wealth to whomever He chooses, with wisdom and justice.
