Allah allowed Adam and his wife to live in Paradise and enjoy its fruits, with the exception of one tree. Shaytan, driven by envy, deceitfully whispered to them, claiming that the tree would grant them the ability to become angels or live forever. He promised them that eating from it would bring them these benefits. Swearing by Allah, Shaytan insisted that he was a sincere advisor and had better knowledge of their situation, encouraging them to follow his guidance. This deception led to their disobedience, and they ate from the forbidden tree.
