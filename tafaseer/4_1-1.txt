Surah An-Nisa was revealed in Madinah. Allah commands mankind to have Taqwa by worshipping Him alone, and reminds them that He created them all from a single soul, Adam, from whom He created Eve. Allah then created many men and women from them, scattering them across the world in various forms. Allah also commands respect for the bonds of kinship, emphasizing the importance of protecting familial ties and being mindful of one's actions, as Allah is always watching over them. The verse encourages kindness toward relatives and adherence to moral conduct, as Allah witnesses all deeds. This reminder about human origins and responsibilities towards one another fosters compassion and mutual respect.
