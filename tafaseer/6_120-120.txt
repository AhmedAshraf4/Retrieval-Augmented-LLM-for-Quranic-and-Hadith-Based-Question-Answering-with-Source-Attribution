"Leave evil, open and secret" refers to all kinds of sins, both public and private. This encompasses any sin, whether committed openly or in secrecy. Allah forbids acts of immorality, whether seen or hidden, as confirmed in the verse, "Say: the things that my Lord has indeed forbidden are evil sins, whether committed openly or secretly." Those who engage in such sinful acts, whether publicly or privately, will face the appropriate recompense for their deeds. The Prophet explained that sin is what causes discomfort in the heart and is something one dislikes others knowing about.
