Tafsir Explanation: Allah describes His servants with Taqwa who, when they experience evil thoughts, anger, or whispers from Shaytan, remember Allah’s punishment and reward. They repent, seek refuge with Allah, and ask for forgiveness, which leads them to become aware of their errors and correct their path. In contrast, the brothers of the devils among mankind continue to encourage others in sin, making the wrong path more appealing and never ceasing in their attempts to lead others astray. These devils persistently incite people to commit evil without stopping, as described in another verse where Shayatin are sent to push the disbelievers to do evil.
