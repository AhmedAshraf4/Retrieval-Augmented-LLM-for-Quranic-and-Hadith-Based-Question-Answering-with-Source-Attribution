Lut, the nephew of Ibrahim, peace be upon them, was sent by Allah to the people of Sodom and the surrounding villages to call them to righteousness and warn them against their wicked practices. The people of Sodom engaged in a sinful act by having sexual relations with men instead of women, an act that had never been committed by any nation before them. Lut condemned their behavior as both evil and ignorant, urging them to abandon this lustful act and return to what Allah created for them, which was lawful marriage with women. He even offered his daughters to them in lawful marriage if they felt the need. However, the people of Sodom rejected his offer, openly declaring that they had no desire for women and only sought the men they had been trying to harm.
