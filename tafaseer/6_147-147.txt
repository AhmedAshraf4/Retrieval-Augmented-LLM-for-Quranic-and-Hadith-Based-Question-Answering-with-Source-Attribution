Allah urges people to seek His vast mercy and follow His Messenger, Muhammad, warning that His wrath will not be averted from the criminal people who oppose him. Allah often combines encouragement with threats in the Qur’an. He emphasizes that He is swift in retribution but also Oft-Forgiving and Merciful. Allah reminds that despite people's wrongdoing, He is full of forgiveness, yet His punishment is severe. Allah declares His ability to forgive sins, accept repentance, and inflict painful torment. This is reiterated in many verses, highlighting the dual nature of Allah’s mercy and justice.
