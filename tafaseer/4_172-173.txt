Tafsir Explanation: The verse explains that neither Al-Masih (Jesus) nor the closest angels are too proud to worship Allah; they are humble servants of Allah. Those who reject His worship out of pride will be gathered on the Day of Resurrection, where Allah will justly judge them. The believers who performed righteous deeds will be rewarded fully, with additional blessings from Allah's mercy and favor. In contrast, those who arrogantly rejected His worship will face painful torment, with no protector or helper beside Allah. They will enter Hell in disgrace and humiliation for their rebellion.
