These verses were revealed when some Jews claimed that Gabriel was their enemy while Michael was their ally. Allah answered that whoever shows enmity toward Gabriel shows enmity toward Allah, because Gabriel brought the Quran down into the heart of Muhammad by Allah’s command, confirming the earlier scriptures and serving as guidance and good news for believers. Gabriel did not act independently, but conveyed revelation only by divine order, so hostility toward him is hostility toward all messengers. The verses also reject the claim that allegiance can be made to one angel while rejecting another, because Allah named Gabriel and Michael together to show that enmity toward either of them is enmity toward Allah, His angels, and His messengers as a whole. Allah declared that whoever takes Allah, His angels, or His messengers as enemies is a disbeliever, and Allah Himself becomes the enemy of such people.
