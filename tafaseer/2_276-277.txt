Allah destroys Riba by either taking it away from those who consume it or depriving them of its blessings, rendering it ultimately harmful. This punishment extends to both this life and the Day of Resurrection. Riba, unlike charity, brings no benefit with Allah, and those who practice it will face a severe fate. In contrast, charity is blessed by Allah and grows like an animal raised by its owner, eventually becoming as great as a mountain. Allah does not love disbelievers or sinners, highlighting that those who engage in Riba demonstrate a lack of appreciation for the lawful blessings Allah provides. Those who believe, perform good deeds, pray, and give charity will receive a great reward from Allah, being free from fear and sorrow on the Day of Resurrection.
