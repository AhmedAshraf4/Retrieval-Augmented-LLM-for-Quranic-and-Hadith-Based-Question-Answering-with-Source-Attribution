The Christians who claim that Jesus is God or the son of God are disbelievers. Despite these claims, Jesus declared himself to be the servant of Allah, from his infancy in the cradle to his adulthood as a Prophet. He proclaimed that Allah is the only God worthy of worship, rejecting any form of divinity for himself or his mother. Those who associate partners with Allah will be denied Paradise and will be sent to the Fire. Allah will not forgive shirk (associating partners with Him), but He forgives other sins to whom He wills. Jesus’ mother, Mary, was a righteous believer, and both she and Jesus were human beings in need of sustenance. The claim that they were gods is false, as they were servants of Allah like other humans. Despite clear evidence against these misguided beliefs, many remain deluded.
