Between Paradise and the Fire, there is a barrier called Al-Araf, a wall with a gate. The residents of Al-Araf are those whose good and bad deeds are equal, preventing them from entering Paradise but also sparing them from the Fire. They remain in this state until Allah judges them. When the people of Al-A`raf see the people of the Fire, they pray to Allah, asking not to be placed with the wrongdoers. They recognize the people of the Fire by their distinct features, but their gathering and pride offer them no protection or advantage.
