Tafsīr Explanation: Allah appointed leaders for the criminals who opposed the Prophets, including Muhammad, as they called to disbelief and hindered the path of Allah. These leaders, living in luxury and defiance, were tasked with plotting evil in their towns, as seen with earlier communities. Their actions led to their destruction, as they defied Allah's commands and were eventually destroyed for their misguidance. Their plotting was a form of deception, and they harmed only themselves by leading others astray. Despite their arrogance and mocking of the Prophets, they admitted the nobility of the Prophet’s lineage and virtue. In the end, those who scorned Allah’s message would face humiliation and severe torment, a punishment for their plots and deceit. On the Day of Resurrection, all hidden deeds will be revealed, and the treachery of those who plotted against the Prophets will be made public, exposing their wrongdoings for all to see.
