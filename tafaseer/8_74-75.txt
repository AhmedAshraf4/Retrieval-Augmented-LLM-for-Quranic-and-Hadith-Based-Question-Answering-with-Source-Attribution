Allah affirmed the loyalty and protection among believers in this life, then highlighted their reward in the Hereafter: forgiveness, the erasure of sins, and honorable, eternal provisions that are abundant and never cause boredom. Those who follow the believers' path of faith and good deeds will be with them in the Hereafter, as exemplified by the foremost in embracing Islam and those who follow them. Allah also declared that kindred by blood are nearer to one another regarding inheritance, a ruling that abrogated earlier ties of treaty or brotherhood. This ruling applies to all relatives, and for those with fixed shares of inheritance, there is no will allowed for them, as every right is allotted by Allah.
