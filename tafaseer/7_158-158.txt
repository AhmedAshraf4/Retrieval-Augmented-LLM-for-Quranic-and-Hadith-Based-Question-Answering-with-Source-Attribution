Muhammad is the final Messenger sent by Allah to all of humanity, both Arabs and non-Arabs, and to the Jinn. Allah commands that people believe in His Messenger and follow his guidance. Muhammad’s mission was universal, as he was sent to all mankind and was described in previous scriptures, including his status as the unlettered Prophet. The Qur'an was revealed to him to warn all people, and those who reject it face the punishment of the Fire. His companions, such as Abu Bakr, recognized his truth when others rejected him. Muhammad was granted several unique blessings, including victory through fear, the legitimacy of war spoils, the earth as a place for prayer, and intercession for his followers on the Day of Judgment. Muhammad believed in Allah and His Words, and his actions were in accordance with the divine guidance he received, urging people to follow him in order to be guided to the Straight Path.
