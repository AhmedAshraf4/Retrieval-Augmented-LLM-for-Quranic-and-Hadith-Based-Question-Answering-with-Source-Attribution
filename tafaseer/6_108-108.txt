Tafsir Explanation: Allah prohibits the believers from insulting the false deities of the idolaters, despite the potential benefit of exposing their falsehood. Insulting their idols could lead to the greater harm of the idolaters retaliating by insulting Allah, the one true God. This prohibition was revealed after the idolaters threatened to insult Allah if Muhammad (PBUH) continued mocking their gods. Allah’s wisdom in this is to avoid a larger evil, as the idolaters’ love for their gods is made fair to them, just as misguidance was made fair to past nations. Ultimately, all will return to Allah, who will judge them and reward them for their deeds, whether good or evil.
