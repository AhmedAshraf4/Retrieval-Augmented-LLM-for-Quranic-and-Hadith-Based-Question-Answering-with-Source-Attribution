Tafsir Explanation: Allah created a share of Hell for many of the Jinn and mankind, prepared by their deeds that align with the actions of its inhabitants. Allah knew their deeds before their creation and had decreed them fifty thousand years before creating the heavens and earth. Those who disbelieve, described as having hearts, eyes, and ears, do not benefit from these faculties because they reject guidance. Allah compares them to cattle, who, despite having senses, do not understand or respond to their shepherd’s call. However, the disbelievers are even more astray than cattle, as they refuse to worship Allah alone, unlike animals that fulfill their purpose in creation. These disbelievers are heedless, unaware of the truth and guidance they reject.
