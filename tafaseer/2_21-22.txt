Allah calls all people to worship Him alone by pointing to direct acts they know and depend on: He brought them into existence after nonexistence, spread the earth as a stable resting place fixed by mountains, raised the sky above them as a protective ceiling, and sent down rain from the sky through clouds when they need it. By that rain, He caused crops, plants, and fruits of different kinds to grow as provision for people and their animals. These acts show that He alone creates, owns, sustains, and provides for life on the earth and beneath the sky. Because these blessings come only from Him, people are forbidden to set up any rivals alongside Him in worship while knowing that He alone created and provides for them.
