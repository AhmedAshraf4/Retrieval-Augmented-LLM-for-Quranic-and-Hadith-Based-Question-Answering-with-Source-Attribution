Tafsir Explanation: Allah criticizes the Jews and Christians who claim to follow their scriptures, the Tawrah and Injil, yet refuse to accept Muhammad when called to follow him, turning away in rejection. They falsely believe they will only endure punishment in the Fire for a limited number of days, specifically seven, a day for each thousand years of this life. This self-deception, rooted in their invented beliefs, leads them to persist in their false creeds. They are unaware that this claim holds no truth and is unsupported by divine authority. Allah warns them of the consequences on the Day of Resurrection, when they will be gathered and held accountable for rejecting His messengers, killing His prophets, and distorting His teachings. On that Day, everyone will receive the full recompense for their deeds, and no injustice will be done to them.
