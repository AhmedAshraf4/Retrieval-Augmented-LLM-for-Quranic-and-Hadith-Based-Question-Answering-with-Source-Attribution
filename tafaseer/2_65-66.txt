You knew the case of the town by the sea that violated the Sabbath. They broke their covenant by using deceit to fish on Saturday. They set nets and artificial pools before the Sabbath so the fish would enter freely on Saturday. The fish came abundantly on that day and were trapped, then they collected them after the Sabbath ended. Because of this deliberate deception, God transformed them from humans into despised monkeys. Their outward actions appeared lawful, but their reality was disobedience, so the punishment matched the crime. They remained in that transformed state briefly and then perished without offspring.
