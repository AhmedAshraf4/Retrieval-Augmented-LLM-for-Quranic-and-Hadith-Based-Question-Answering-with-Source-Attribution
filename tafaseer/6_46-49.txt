Allah warns that if He were to take away the senses of hearing and sight, as He gave them to humans, no one but Allah could restore them. This is emphasized by His statement about the ownership of hearing and sight, illustrating His sole control over these faculties. Allah further explains that He may seal the hearts of disbelievers, preventing them from benefiting from these senses in understanding the truth. Despite the clear signs of Allah's power and Oneness, people still turn away from the truth and hinder others from it. Allah then challenges them, asking if anyone besides Him can send His punishment suddenly, either at night or in the day, making it clear that only the wrongdoers will face destruction. The righteous, who believe in Allah and follow the guidance of the Messengers, will not fear or grieve, as Allah will protect them. On the other hand, those who reject His signs and rebel will be punished for their disobedience.
