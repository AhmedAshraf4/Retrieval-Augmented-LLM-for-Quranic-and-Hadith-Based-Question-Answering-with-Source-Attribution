Tafsir Explanation: Allah guides whom He wills by opening their hearts to Islam, making it easy for them to embrace faith. This guidance strengthens their resolve, allowing them to feel the light of faith in their hearts. In contrast, those Allah chooses to misguide have their hearts closed and constricted, unable to accept faith. This is compared to the difficulty of climbing to the sky, illustrating the impossibility of faith entering their hearts. The disbeliever’s heart is impenetrable, just as climbing to the sky is beyond human capability. As a result, Allah appoints Satan to misguide such individuals, leading them further away from the truth. This state of being is referred to as "Rijs," which signifies torment or the presence of Satan’s influence in their hearts, preventing them from finding guidance.
