Tafsir Explanation: Whenever a Messenger brought a message they disliked, some of them called him a liar, while others killed him. They believed there would be no punishment for their actions, thinking they would not face consequences for their evil. As a result, they were blinded to the truth and deaf to it, making them incapable of being guided. Despite this, Allah forgave them, but many continued in their blindness and deafness. Allah sees all their deeds and knows who among them deserves guidance and who deserves misguidance.
