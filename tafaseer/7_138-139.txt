After the Children of Israel crossed the Sea and witnessed Allah's power, they encountered a people worshipping idols. Some of the Israelites, influenced by the idol worship they saw, asked Moses to make them a god like the ones the others had. Moses responded by rebuking them, calling them ignorant for asking such a thing, as they were unaware of Allah’s greatness and His purity from partners. He warned them that the idol worshippers would be destroyed for their actions, and all their efforts would be in vain. This incident was later echoed when the Companions of the Prophet Muhammad, on their way to the battle of Hunayn, requested the same from him upon seeing a tree the disbelievers used for idol worship. The Prophet reminded them of the mistake the Children of Israel had made with Moses, emphasizing that such behavior was ignorant and led to destruction.
