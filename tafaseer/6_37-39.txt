Tafsir Explanation: The idolators demanded a miracle from Muhammad, each proposing specific signs, such as causing a spring to gush forth from the earth. Although Allah is capable of sending such signs, He delays their fulfillment out of wisdom. If a sign of their choosing were given and they still refused to believe, their punishment would be hastened, as seen with past nations. Allah’s knowledge encompasses all creatures, including every living being, bird, and insect, each of which has its sustenance and destined end recorded. On the Day of Resurrection, all creatures will be gathered, and justice will prevail, with even the smallest retribution. Those who reject Allah’s signs are compared to the deaf, mute, and blind, unable to find guidance, as their ignorance leads them to darkness. Allah guides whom He wills and misguides whom He wills, for His will governs all.
