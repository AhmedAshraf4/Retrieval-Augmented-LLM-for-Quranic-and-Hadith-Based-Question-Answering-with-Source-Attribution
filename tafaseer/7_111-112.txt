Tafsir Explanation: Pharaoh instructed to delay Moses and summoned magicians from his kingdom’s provinces, as magic was widely practiced and believed to be a potential match for the miracles Moses was performing. Pharaoh and his advisers, misunderstanding Moses' signs, thought that his miracles were a form of magic similar to their own, so they gathered magicians to counteract him. They agreed to meet on the day of the festival, where both sides would have an equal opportunity. Moses appointed the day of the festival and the time when the people would gather in the morning. Pharaoh withdrew, made his preparations, and returned to face Moses.
