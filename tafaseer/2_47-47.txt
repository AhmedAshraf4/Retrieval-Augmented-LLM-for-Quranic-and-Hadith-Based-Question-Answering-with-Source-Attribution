“O Children of Israel, remember My favor which I bestowed upon you, and that I preferred you over the worlds.” This statement recalls the specific favors given to the Children of Israel through their ancestors, including that God sent messengers from among them, revealed scriptures to them, and granted them kingship, advantages not given to other nations living at the same time. The preference refers to these concrete gifts of prophethood, revelation, and authority during their historical periods, not an absolute superiority over all later communities. Their distinction applied to their own eras, while later God declared the community of Muhammad to be better than them and all others through obedience, belief, and command of good.
