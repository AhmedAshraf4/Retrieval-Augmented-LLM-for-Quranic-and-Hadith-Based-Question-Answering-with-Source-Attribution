Tafsir Explanation: Allah testifies that He is the only true God, the Lord of all creation, independent of all needs, and the source of justice. The angels and those with knowledge also testify to His singularity, emphasizing His absolute truth, might, and wisdom. Islam is the only accepted religion with Allah, and those who reject it, including Jews and Christians after receiving the scriptures, differ due to rivalry and corruption. Allah warns that anyone who disbelieves in His signs will face swift reckoning. If others dispute Muhammad's message, he is commanded to declare his submission to Allah alone, and those who follow him do the same. Muhammad's message is universal, calling all people, including the People of the Book and idolaters, to submit to Allah. Allah's guidance is ultimately His decision, and He sees all His servants, with no one able to question His will. Muhammad was sent to all of humanity, as clearly indicated in both the Qur'an and Hadith.
