Tafsir Explanation: Allah explains that He reveals His signs clearly, guiding those who seek the truth while exposing the path of the criminals who defy the Prophets. The Prophet Muhammad, though asked by his people for immediate punishment, clarifies that he does not control the torment they demand. Allah alone holds the power to decide the timing of punishment, showing His wisdom in dealing with wrongdoers. The Prophet’s patience is evident in his choice to ask for respite for the disbelievers, hoping that they might eventually worship Allah alone. This is illustrated by the incident where the Angel of the Mountains offered to crush the disbelievers with two mountains, but the Prophet chose mercy. Allah's knowledge encompasses all things, including the unseen, such as the knowledge of the Hour, rain, what is in the wombs, and even the tiniest movements in the universe, emphasizing His total control and awareness over all creation.
