The verse condemns those who fabricate lies against Allah, particularly those who claim to have received divine revelation when they have not, or who assert they can reveal something similar to Allah's message. This refers to individuals like Musaylimah Al-Kadhdhab, who falsely claimed prophethood. Allah then describes the torment awaiting such wrongdoers at the moment of death, when the angels stretch forth their hands, beating them as they take their souls, announcing their punishment for lying about Allah. On the Day of Resurrection, people will stand before Allah alone, having left behind all their worldly possessions. Allah will rebuke the disbelievers for their false deities, revealing their idols' inability to intercede, and all relationships and hopes in those false gods will vanish. They will realize that their false beliefs and claims about Allah have led to their downfall.
