This verse addresses those who divide their religion into sects, particularly the Jews and Christians, who split into various factions before the advent of Muhammad. When Muhammad was sent, Allah made it clear that those who divide their religion and form sects are not connected to him in any way. Allah's Messenger was sent with the religion of truth, a unified law free from contradictions, to make it dominant over all other religions. Those who break into sects or follow misguidance are not part of the Messenger’s path, which is solely about worshipping Allah without partners. All paths diverging from this are misguidance and ignorance. Allah has stated that these people’s matters are for Him to decide, and He will inform them of their actions on the Day of Judgment, where He will justly judge all, including those who followed different religions.
