Allah created all mankind from Adam, peace be upon him, and from him, his wife Hawwa', and from them, people began to spread across the earth. Allah created spouses from among themselves to provide comfort and affection, as seen in the intimate bond between a husband and wife. In the early stages of pregnancy, the woman carries the fetus lightly, not feeling much, until it becomes noticeable as it grows heavier. Adam and Hawwa' prayed to Allah, asking for a righteous child, fearing that their child might not be human. When Allah granted them a healthy child, they wrongfully ascribed partners to Him, showing that many of Adam's descendants, not Adam and Hawwa' themselves, fell into shirk by turning to idol worship, such as among the Jews and Christians.
