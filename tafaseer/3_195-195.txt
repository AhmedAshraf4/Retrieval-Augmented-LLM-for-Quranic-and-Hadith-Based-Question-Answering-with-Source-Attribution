Allah accepted the supplication of those who migrated for His sake, whether male or female, assuring that no good deed would be lost. He rewards all equally, regardless of gender, as they are equal in His eyes. The verse highlights the emigration of believers who left their homes and loved ones to escape persecution for their faith. They were driven out by the disbelievers, endured harm, and faced suffering for their belief in Allah. The highest rank is attained by those who fought and were martyred in His cause, as they receive complete forgiveness for their sins, except for their debts. Allah promises to expiate their sins and grant them entry into Paradise, where rivers flow beneath their gardens, offering eternal rewards beyond human imagination. This reward is from Allah, the Mighty, who gives the best of rewards to those who perform righteous deeds.
