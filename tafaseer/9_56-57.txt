Allah describes the hypocrites' fear and anxiety, showing that while they swear allegiance to the believers, they are not truly part of them. They are people driven by fear, and this compels them to make false oaths. If they could find a place of refuge, such as a fort, cave, or tunnel, they would swiftly flee there to avoid associating with the Muslims. They feel sorrow when they see Muslims prosper, and their preference is to stay away from them.
