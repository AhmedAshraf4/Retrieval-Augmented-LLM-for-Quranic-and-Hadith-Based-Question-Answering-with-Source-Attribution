Tafsir Explanation: This verse addresses the ruling on Al-Kalalah, the situation where a person dies without descendants or ascendants. The final verse revealed clarifies the inheritance of those in this situation, particularly a man who dies leaving behind no children or parents. If he has a sister, she inherits half of his estate. If there are two or more sisters, they share two-thirds of the inheritance. In the case of a brother inheriting from a sister with no children, he takes all of her inheritance if she has no surviving parents. The verse also outlines the rule that the male heirs generally receive double the share of the female heirs. The Prophet’s companion Umar sought clarification on the meaning of Kalalah, as he found it confusing, but was reassured by the Prophet's guidance, which aligned with the rulings in the verse.
