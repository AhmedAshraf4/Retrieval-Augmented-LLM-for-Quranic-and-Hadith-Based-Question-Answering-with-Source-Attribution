Allah sent down a clear and undeniable proof to all people, a manifest light that leads to the Truth, which is the Qur'an. Those who believe in Allah and rely on Him, worshiping Him and holding fast to the Qur'an, will be admitted to His mercy and grace. Allah will grant them Paradise, increase their rewards, and elevate their ranks as a favor from Him. He will also guide them to Himself through a straight path, a way free from deviation or evil, which is the path of righteousness both in this life and the Hereafter, leading to the gardens of Paradise.
