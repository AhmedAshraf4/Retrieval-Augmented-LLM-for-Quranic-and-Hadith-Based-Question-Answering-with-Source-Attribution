Tafsir Explanation: The gates of Heaven will not open for those who deny Allah's signs, meaning their good deeds and supplications will not ascend to the heavens. The souls of disbelievers will not be accepted by the angels, who will call them by the worst names they were known by in life as their souls ascend. When these souls reach the lower Heaven, the angels will request the gates to be opened for them, but they will remain closed. The Prophet Muhammad described this in a Hadith, illustrating that the soul of a wicked person will be denied entry, aligning with the statement that "the gates of heaven will not be opened for them." The disbelievers will also not enter Paradise until a male camel passes through the eye of a needle, symbolizing the impossibility of their entry. Their fate will be to have beds from the Fire and coverings above them, a punishment for the wrongdoers, as Allah recompenses them for their deeds.
