Tafsir Explanation: Allah does not wrong anyone, not even by the weight of a mustard seed or a speck of dust. On the Day of Resurrection, every action, whether good or bad, will be shown to each person. If the action is good, Allah will reward and multiply it. Even the smallest good deed, like a mustard seed, will be rewarded, and any evil deed, no matter how small, will be seen as well. For the disbelievers, their punishment may be lessened, but they will never escape Hell. The Prophet Muhammad’s intercession will bring some, like his uncle Abu Talib, to a lighter punishment. The reward for good deeds can be vast, with Allah multiplying it greatly, even to millions of times. The Day of Judgment will be a time of horror for the disbelievers, who will wish to be swallowed by the earth to avoid the disgrace. They will try to deny their sins but will be unable to hide anything, as their hands and feet will testify against them.
