They attribute their fabrications to Allah, fully aware of their deceit. The distortion occurs through misinterpretation and deliberate additions or changes, but the original Books of Allah, like the Torah and the Gospel, remain unchanged and preserved. These altered texts are then presented as divine revelation, although they are not from Allah. While the original scriptures are intact, the current versions in the hands of the People of the Book have been heavily corrupted, with numerous errors, additions, deletions, and misinterpretations, particularly in translations that reflect incorrect understandings.
