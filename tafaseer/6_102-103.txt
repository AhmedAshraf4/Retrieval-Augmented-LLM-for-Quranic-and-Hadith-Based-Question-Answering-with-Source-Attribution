Tafsir Explanation: Allah is the Creator of all things, and none has the right to be worshipped except Him. He has no offspring, wife, equal, or rival. Allah is the Guardian and Disposer of all affairs, providing for and protecting everything. No vision can grasp Him in this life, but He sees and comprehends all vision. The Prophet Muhammad affirmed that Allah cannot be seen in this world, citing the verse "No vision can grasp Him, but His grasp is over all vision." In the Hereafter, however, believers will see Allah as He wills. When Prophet Moses asked to see Allah, Allah made the mountain collapse, causing Moses to fall unconscious, later repenting and declaring himself the first believer. This incident shows that no living being can withstand seeing Allah in this life, but believers will behold Him in the Hereafter, in a manner that preserves His might and grace. Allah's knowledge encompasses all vision, as He is the Creator, well-acquainted with everything.
