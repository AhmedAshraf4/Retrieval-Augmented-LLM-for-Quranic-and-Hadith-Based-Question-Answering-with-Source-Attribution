“And do not mix truth with falsehood or conceal the truth while you know it. Establish prayer, give charity, and bow with those who bow.” The address targets the Jews and the People of the Book, forbidding them from deliberately combining revealed truth with false claims and from hiding what they knew to be true, specifically their knowledge of Muhammad and his description recorded in their scriptures, which they concealed while spreading falsehood. They were commanded to make the truth clear rather than distort it. They were also ordered to establish the prescribed prayer, to give the required charity to Muhammad, and to bow physically with those who bow, meaning to pray behind the Prophet and join the believers in communal worship, placing themselves within the community of Muhammad through the same acts of prayer, charity, and submission.
