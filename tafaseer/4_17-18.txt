Repentance is accepted by Allah until death approaches, specifically until the soul reaches the throat. A person who repents before this moment, even if they were unaware or sinful, will be forgiven. However, once death nears and the soul begins to depart, repentance is no longer accepted, nor is there a way to escape the inevitable end. This is why Allah does not accept repentance from those who persist in sin until death confronts them, nor from those who disbelieve until they see the punishment. For the disbeliever, even the offer of a ransom will not be accepted if they die in disbelief. The end of the disbeliever’s repentance comes when their soul departs while they are still a polytheist.
