Tafsir Explanation: After Musa went to meet Allah on Mount Tur, his people were misled by As-Samiri, who made a calf from the ornaments they borrowed from the Copts. He threw dust from the horse of the Angel Jibril into the calf, which then appeared to moo. The Israelites began worshipping it, declaring it their god and the god of Musa, claiming that Musa had forgotten it. Allah condemned their misguided worship, pointing out that the calf could neither speak nor guide them, nor offer them any benefit. When the Israelites realized their error and felt regret, they sought Allah’s mercy, acknowledging their sin and asking for His forgiveness, fearing they would become among the losers.
