He created for you all that is on the earth; then He turned to the heaven while it was smoke and formed it into seven heavens, and He knows all things. The earth was created first with all that is in it prepared for human use. From it arose smoke, which was the state of the heaven when He turned to it. He then fashioned the heaven into seven distinct heavens, one above another, each assigned its ordered function. After the heavens were completed, the earth was spread out by bringing its stored elements to the surface: water emerged, vegetation grew, and mountains were fixed in place. This sequence shows initial creation of the earth, formation of the heavens from smoke, and later spreading of the earth to produce sustenance, all occurring under complete divine knowledge.
