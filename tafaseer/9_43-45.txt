Tafsir Explanation: The Prophet was criticized, though mildly, for granting permission to the Hypocrites to stay behind from battle. The criticism began with forgiveness, as it was mentioned, "May Allah forgive you. Why did you grant them leave?" Allah later permitted the Prophet to allow some to stay behind if they sought permission, as seen in Surah An-Nur. The verse exposed those who asked permission to stay behind without a valid excuse, revealing the true believers who were eager to participate in Jihad and those who were unwilling. True believers did not seek to be exempted from fighting and considered Jihad an act of worship, acting with obedience. In contrast, those who sought exemption were marked by doubt and disbelief in the Hereafter, wavering in their stance and showing no firm commitment. Those who are misled by Allah will never find a way to true guidance.
