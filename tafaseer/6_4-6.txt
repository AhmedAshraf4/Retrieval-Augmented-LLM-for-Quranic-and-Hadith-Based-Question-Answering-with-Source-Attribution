Tafsir Explanation: The stubborn polytheists reject every sign and proof of Allah's Uniqueness and the truth of His Messengers, disregarding them without contemplation. They mock the truth, but they will soon face the consequences of their denial. They will realize the truth of what they mocked and suffer the end they earned for their actions. Allah warns them of the same fate that befell previous nations who were more powerful, wealthier, and numerous. These nations enjoyed abundant wealth, children, and resources, and Allah sent them frequent rains and flowing rivers, yet they were destroyed due to their sins. After their destruction, new generations arose, but they repeated the same errors and faced the same end. The idolators are reminded that they are not dearer to Allah than those previous nations, and their rejection of the Messenger places them in greater danger of punishment, if not for Allah's mercy.
