Tafsir Explanation: Allah emphasized that the victory at Badr was not due to the strength or number of the believers, but rather it was His will that granted them triumph. The Prophet Muhammad, when surrounded, threw a handful of sand at the disbelievers, declaring their faces to be humiliated. This act, although seemingly small, was made effective by Allah, who caused the sand to strike their eyes and distract them. Victory and success come from Allah alone, as He can make a small group overcome a large one by His permission. The believers were tested and reminded of Allah's favor in granting them victory over a far superior enemy. Allah also promised to weaken the plots of the disbelievers, ensuring their eventual defeat and destruction.
