Allah states that most people are misguided, and their way is based on conjecture and delusion. Despite their misguided path, they remain doubtful and rely on wishful thinking, as they follow only assumptions and lies. Allah knows best who is led astray and who is rightly guided, and He facilitates their paths according to His will. This is part of Allah's decree, with each person being guided or misled as He intends, in fulfillment of His decision.
