Tafsir Explanation: The Jews falsely accused Allah of being a miser, claiming His hand is tied and that He is poor while they are rich. This accusation implied that Allah does not spend from His vast bounty, a statement refuted by Allah, who has unlimited wealth and freely gives as He wills. Allah is not bound by their fabrications. The Jews' hostility and denial of the truth, despite the revelations sent to Muhammad, only increased their rebellion and disbelief. Allah's bounty is ever-expanding, and had the People of the Book followed the teachings of their scriptures, they would have been blessed with immense provisions from the heavens and the earth. However, most of them failed to act on their scriptures, with only a few following the right path, leading to their punishment. If they had believed and followed the guidance of the Qur'an, their sins would have been forgiven, and they would have been granted paradise.
