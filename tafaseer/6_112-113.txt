Tafsir Explanation: Allah has made enemies for every Prophet, including Muhammad, who will oppose and harm them. Just as earlier Messengers faced denial and harm with patience, so will Muhammad. These enemies are both among the devils of mankind and Jinn, who inspire each other with deceptive and false speech. Despite their opposition, Allah allows this to occur by His will. The verse advises patience in the face of their harm and trust in Allah’s protection. The disbelievers are those who accept this false speech and find satisfaction in it, as they do not believe in the Hereafter. They are free to act as they wish, but their actions will lead to consequences.
