The people of Pharaoh were tested with years of drought and famine, which caused a severe lack of produce, including dates that only produced a single fruit. When prosperity and good seasons came, they claimed it was their right. However, when drought and hardship afflicted them, they blamed Moses and his followers, believing that their misfortune was caused by them. Allah clarified that their misfortunes were from Him, and their superstitions were misplaced, for their fate was determined by Allah, but most of them did not understand this.
