Tafsir Explanation: Allah commands the Prophet Muhammad to deliver the full message He has revealed to him, a task the Prophet fulfilled completely. The Prophet's integrity in conveying the message is emphasized, with a strong statement that if he had hidden even a single verse, he would have failed in his mission. Allah assures the Prophet that He will protect him from harm by people, providing safety as he fulfills his duty. This protection came after the Prophet had been guarded by his companions during the early days in Madinah. Once this verse was revealed, the Prophet declared that Allah’s protection was guaranteed, and no harm would come to him. Allah also reminds Muhammad that while his duty is to convey the message, guidance and misguidance are solely in His hands, and He does not guide those who disbelieve.
