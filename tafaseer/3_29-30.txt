Allah knows everything, both the hidden and the apparent, in every moment and place. His knowledge encompasses all that is in the heavens and the earth, and nothing escapes His observation, not even the weight of an atom. Allah is fully capable of doing all things, and His power extends over everything. This knowledge serves as a warning to His servants, urging them to avoid His prohibitions, as He is aware of all their actions and has the power to swiftly punish. On the Day of Resurrection, everyone will face their deeds; the good they did will bring them joy, while the evil will bring them regret. Those who see their evil deeds will wish they could distance themselves from them, even wishing to be far from the devil who encouraged their misdeeds. Allah warns them against His punishment but also reassures them with His mercy and kindness, urging them to stay on His straight path.
