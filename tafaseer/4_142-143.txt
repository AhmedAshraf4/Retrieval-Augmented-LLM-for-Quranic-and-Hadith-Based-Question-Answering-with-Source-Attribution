Tafsir Explanation: The hypocrites attempt to deceive Allah, thinking that their outward conformity to Islam will shield them from His judgment. They show laziness in their prayers and perform them to be seen by others, not from genuine belief or humility. When they stand for prayer, they do so with negligence and inattentiveness. They sway between faith and disbelief, aligning with believers outwardly but harboring disbelief inwardly. Allah, in His wisdom, deceives them further, preventing them from reaching the truth, both in this life and in the hereafter. On the Day of Judgment, the hypocrites will swear they were righteous, but their claims will not benefit them. They will be left without a guiding friend, as Allah has led them astray, and no one can guide whom He has misdirected.
