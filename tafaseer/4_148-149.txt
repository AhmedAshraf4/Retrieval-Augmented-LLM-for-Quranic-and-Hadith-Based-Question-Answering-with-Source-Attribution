Allah does not like the public utterance of evil unless one has been wronged, in which case the wronged individual may invoke Allah against the one who harmed them. However, it is better to show patience. If someone curses you, retaliation is allowed, but if they lie about you, you should not lie in return. Allah permits the wronged person to seek justice without exceeding limits. Allah values forgiveness and pardoning, which brings people closer to Him and increases their reward. Allah, though capable of punishment, forgives and pardons His servants. His angels praise Him for His forbearance despite His perfect knowledge and ability to punish. Furthermore, charity does not diminish wealth, and forgiving others brings greater honor, just as humbling oneself for Allah leads to elevation in status.
