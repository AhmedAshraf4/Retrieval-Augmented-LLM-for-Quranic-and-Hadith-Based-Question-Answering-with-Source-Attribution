Tafsir Explanation: Musa fasted and worshiped Allah for forty days. Allah reminded the Children of Israel of the guidance He had sent to them through Musa, revealing the Tawrah, which contained their laws and legislation. Allah initially appointed a period of thirty nights for Musa, during which he fasted. Afterward, Musa cleansed his teeth with a twig, and Allah commanded him to complete ten additional days, making the total forty. Once this period ended, Musa prepared to return to Mount Tur and left his brother Harun in charge of the Children of Israel, instructing him to act wisely and avoid mischief. Harun, a noble and honorable Prophet, was entrusted with this responsibility.
