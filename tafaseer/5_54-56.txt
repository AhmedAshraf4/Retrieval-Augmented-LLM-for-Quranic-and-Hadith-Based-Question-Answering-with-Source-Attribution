Tafsīr Explanation: Allah warns that those who abandon His religion and law will be replaced by a people who are more righteous and stronger in their faith. This serves as a reminder of Allah’s power to remove and replace anyone He wills. Believers are described as humble towards each other and stern with disbelievers, reflecting their commitment to Allah’s commands. The Prophet Muhammad and his companions exemplify this attitude, showing mercy to fellow believers and firmness against enemies. True believers do not fear blame when fulfilling their duties for Allah’s sake, whether in worship, fighting for His cause, or enforcing righteousness. The faithful are those who support Allah, His Messenger, and other believers, establishing prayer, paying Zakah, and living in accordance with Allah’s laws. Those who align themselves with Allah and His Messenger will be victorious, as the party of Allah, aided by divine support, will succeed in this life and the Hereafter.
