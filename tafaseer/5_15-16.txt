Tafsir Explanation: Allah sent His Messenger Muhammad with clear guidance and the religion of truth for all people, both Arabs and non-Arabs, literate and illiterate. He was sent to reveal the truth, exposing the distortions and falsehoods that people had hidden in their scriptures. The Prophet clarified what had been altered and ignored what held no benefit. The Qur'an, a light and a plain Book, was revealed to guide those who seek Allah’s pleasure to paths of peace, bringing them out of darkness into light, and guiding them to the straight path. Through this guidance, Allah protects them from harm and leads them to the most righteous way of life.
