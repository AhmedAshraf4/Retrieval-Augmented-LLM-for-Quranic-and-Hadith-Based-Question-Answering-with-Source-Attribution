Tafsir Explanation: When the small group of faithful under Talut's command faced the large army led by Jalut, they invoked Allah for patience, to keep their feet firm, and for victory over the disbelieving people. Allah granted their request, allowing them to defeat the enemy by His aid. Dawud killed Jalut, fulfilling the prophecy that the one who killed him would marry Talut's daughter and share in his kingship. Talut kept his promise, and Dawud was later granted the kingdom and prophethood by Allah. Allah taught him the knowledge He willed. Allah also stated that if He did not use one group of people to check another, the earth would be filled with corruption, as seen when Talut and Dawud protected the Children of Israel. Allah’s mercy and wisdom ensure the protection of His creation. The verses were recited to Muhammad in truth, affirming his prophethood.
