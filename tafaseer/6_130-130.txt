Tafsir Explanation: On the Day of Resurrection, Allah will ask the disbelieving Jinn and humans, who had previously admitted that messengers were sent to them, if the messages were delivered to them. The messengers were from among mankind, not the Jinn, and they conveyed Allah’s messages to the people, warning them of the coming Day. Despite this, the disbelievers were deceived by the attractions of worldly life, leading them to reject the messengers and deny their miracles. On that Day, they will bear witness against themselves, acknowledging their disbelief and the truth of the warning they received. They will admit that the messengers conveyed Allah’s verses and warned them of the meeting with Him, but their rejection will be due to their attachment to the transient life of this world.
