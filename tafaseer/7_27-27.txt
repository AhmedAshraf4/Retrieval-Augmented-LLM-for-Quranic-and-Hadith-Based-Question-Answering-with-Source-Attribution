Allah warns the Children of Adam about Iblis and his followers, highlighting Iblis' long-standing hatred for Adam. Iblis sought to have Adam removed from Paradise, a place of ease, to the hardships of this life. He also exposed Adam's private parts, which had previously been concealed. This act reveals Iblis' deep animosity towards Adam and his descendants. Allah similarly condemns those who choose Iblis and his offspring as protectors instead of Allah, noting that they are enemies to humans, and such a choice is evil for the wrongdoers.
