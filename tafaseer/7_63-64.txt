Tafsir Explanation: Allah sent revelation to a man from among the people as a mercy and compassion to warn them from His torment and guide them to avoid associating others with Him, offering them His mercy. Despite this, the people rejected and opposed him, and only a few believed. Allah saved the prophet and those who believed in him by rescuing them in the ark, while those who denied Allah's signs were drowned and cast into the Fire due to their sins. They found no help except from Allah. The disbelievers were blind to the truth, unable to recognize or find their way to it. This is Allah’s established way: He grants victory to His messengers and those who fear Him, as seen in the destruction of the disbelievers and the salvation of the believers.
