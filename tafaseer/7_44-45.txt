Tafsir Explanation: The people of Hell will be confronted with anguish after anguish. When they are in Hell, they will be chastised by the angels and reminded of the promises and warnings from Allah. The people of Paradise will confirm that Allah's promises were true, and the people of Hell will admit that they too have found their Lord's promises true. In the Hereafter, they will be shown the consequences of their disbelief and their actions, with angels warning them that the Fire they now endure is the very one they denied in their lives. They will be reminded that no matter their patience or impatience, their punishment will be the same because it is a result of their deeds. The Messenger of Allah addressed the disbelievers at Badr, questioning if they had found the reality of Allah's promises, while confirming that he had seen the truth of his own promises. A crier will announce the curse of Allah upon those who hindered others from His path and tried to distort it, leading people away from His guidance. These wrongdoers, who disbelieved in the Hereafter and in the reckoning, will face their punishment, for their actions were the worst due to their denial of the afterlife and their disregard for consequences.
