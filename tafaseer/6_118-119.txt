Tafsir Explanation: Allah permits His servants to eat slaughtered animals on which His Name was mentioned during the slaughtering. This excludes animals over which His Name was not mentioned, a practice followed by the Quraysh, who ate dead animals and those slaughtered for idols. Allah emphasizes that His servants are allowed to consume what is sacrificed in His Name, as He has clearly detailed what is forbidden. However, in cases of necessity, one may eat what is available. Allah condemns the misguided practices of the idolators, who, due to ignorance, followed their desires, including eating dead animals or those sacrificed to other than Allah. He knows the transgressions of those who lead others astray.
