The disbelievers were asked if they were waiting for one of two things: martyrdom or victory over the believers. The believers, in turn, were awaiting Allah’s punishment upon them or victory through their own hands, such as capture or killing. They were told to wait, as the believers were also waiting for them. The disbelievers were further told that any charity they give would not be accepted, as they were a rebellious people. Their disbelief in Allah and His Messenger, their laziness in prayer, and their unwillingness to give charity prevented the acceptance of their deeds. Allah only accepts deeds from those who have faith and are sincere in their actions, as He is pure and only accepts what is pure.
