Allah forbids believers from taking disbelievers or hypocrites as allies, as they seek to harm the believers and expose their secrets. Hypocrites pretend to be loyal but secretly despise Islam, wishing ill upon the believers, as shown by their behavior. They express false love for the believers but conceal hatred in their hearts. When the believers are successful, the hypocrites feel distressed, and when the believers face difficulty, they take pleasure in it. Allah commands the believers to remain patient and fear Him, for no harm will come from the hypocrites if they do so. Allah is fully aware of the secret intentions in people's hearts, and no matter how much the hypocrites rage, Allah will ensure His favor reaches the believers. Even in times of loss or hardship, as experienced in the Battle of Uhud, the believers are to trust Allah, for He will protect them from harm and guide them to success.
