Tafsir Explanation: Allah reminds the believers of their past state of weakness and subjugation, which He transformed into strength and triumph. When they were few, oppressed, and fearing persecution in Makkah, Allah granted them strength, victory, and sustenance. He allowed them to migrate to Al-Madinah, where they found refuge with the Ansar, who supported them with wealth and lives in obedience to Allah. Through Islam, Allah granted them dominance and provision, lifting them from their previous state of misery and weakness. Allah commands them to be grateful for His blessings, as gratitude brings even more bounties.
