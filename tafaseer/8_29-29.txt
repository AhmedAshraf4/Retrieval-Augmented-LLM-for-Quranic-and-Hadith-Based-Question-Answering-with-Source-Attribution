Furqan, mentioned in the verse, refers to a means of salvation or aid, both in this life and the Hereafter, guiding believers to distinguish between truth and falsehood. It represents a triumph for those who fear Allah, obeying His commands and avoiding His prohibitions. Such individuals are granted guidance, safety, and happiness, both in the worldly life and the Hereafter. Their sins are forgiven and covered, and they are led towards Allah’s immense rewards. This concept is fulfilled by Allah’s promise to believers who fear Him and believe in His Messenger, offering them double mercy, light to guide their steps, and forgiveness.
