Moses was asked whether he or the magicians would throw first. He chose to let them throw first, so the people would witness their magic before the truth was revealed. When the magicians threw their ropes and sticks, they appeared to move, creating the illusion of real magic and striking fear into the people. However, when Moses threw his staff, it swallowed their tricks, proving that their magic was nothing but an illusion. Moses was initially fearful, but Allah reassured him, stating that he would have the upper hand and that the magicians' tricks would not succeed.
