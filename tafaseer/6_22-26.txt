On the Day of Resurrection, Allah will question the polytheists about the idols and rivals they worshipped instead of Him. He will ask, "Where are the partners you used to claim?" They will deny their association with any partners, but their lies will be exposed, and the false deities they invoked will abandon them. Despite hearing the Qur'an, many disbelievers fail to understand its message due to the veils Allah has placed over their hearts and the deafness in their ears. Even when they witness clear signs and miracles, they refuse to believe. When they argue with Prophet Muhammad, they accuse him of merely repeating ancient tales. They not only reject the truth themselves but also hinder others from following it. In doing so, they harm only themselves, yet they are unaware of it.
