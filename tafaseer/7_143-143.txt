When Musa spoke directly to Allah, he requested to see Him. Allah responded that no living being can see Him and survive. To demonstrate this, Allah revealed a part of Himself to a mountain, causing it to collapse to dust, and Musa fell unconscious. After regaining consciousness, Musa glorified Allah and repented for asking to see Him. He declared himself the first believer among the Children of Israel, acknowledging that no one could see Allah in this life. The event is further exemplified in a hadith where the Prophet Muhammad mentioned that on the Day of Judgment, he would be the first to wake after the people are struck unconscious, finding Musa holding onto a pillar of the Throne.
