Seek help through patience and prayer to attain benefit in this life and the Hereafter. Patience here refers to restraining oneself, including fasting and avoiding prohibited acts. Prayer is a primary means of holding firmly to obedience and restraining wrongdoing. Prayer is difficult and burdensome, except for those who are humble. These are the ones who are certain that they will meet their Lord, be gathered before Him, and return to Him for judgment. Because they are sure of this return and reckoning, obedience becomes easy for them and avoidance of prohibitions becomes manageable.
