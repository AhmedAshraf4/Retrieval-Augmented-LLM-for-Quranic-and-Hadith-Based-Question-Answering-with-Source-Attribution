These are people who deliberately exchanged guidance for misguidance, choosing error instead of what would lead them rightly. This includes those who first believed and then rejected belief, causing their hearts to become sealed, and those who were shown guidance but consciously preferred deviation over it. Their action is described as a trade in which guidance was given up to acquire misguidance, resulting in complete loss. Their transaction produced no profit, because abandoning guidance led them away from righteousness and right direction. As a result, they left guidance for deviation, unity for division, safety for fear, and the established prophetic way for innovation, so they neither gained benefit nor remained rightly guided.
