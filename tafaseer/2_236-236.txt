Allah allows divorce before consummating the marriage, even if the dowry is deferred. The term "touched" in the verse refers to sexual intercourse. The husband may divorce his wife before consummation, and in this case, he is required to provide her with a reasonable gift. This gift should be proportional to his means, whether he is rich or poor, compensating her for the loss. A practical example is when the Prophet married Umaymah bint Sharahil, but she was displeased when he extended his hand to her. In response, the Prophet instructed that she be given provisions and a gift of two garments.
