This verse refers to a community that stands firm in truth, both in speech and action, guiding others with the truth and establishing justice through adherence and judgment. It is interpreted to refer to the Ummah of Muhammad (peace be upon him). A group from his Ummah will remain steadfast on the truth, unaffected by those who oppose or abandon them, until the Last Hour arrives. These people are specifically said to be found in Ash-Sham (Greater Syria).
