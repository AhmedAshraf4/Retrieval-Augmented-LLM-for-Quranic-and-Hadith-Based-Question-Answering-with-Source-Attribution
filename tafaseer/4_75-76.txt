Tafsir Explanation: Allah encouraged the believers to engage in Jihad to defend the oppressed Muslims in Makkah, who were suffering due to their forced stay in the town. They cried out to Allah, asking for rescue from the oppression of Makkah, as He had described towns stronger than it in past instances. The oppressed asked for a protector and helper from Allah. Allah then distinguished between the believers, who fight in His cause, and the disbelievers, who fight for Shaytan, urging the believers to fight against the allies of Shaytan, whose plots are weak. When the command for fighting was issued, some of the believers feared people as they feared Allah or even more. They questioned why fighting was decreed upon them, asking for a delay, but were reminded that the pleasures of this world are brief and that the Hereafter is better for those who fear Allah. Allah reminded them that death will reach them wherever they are, even in fortified towers, and that both good and bad events come from Allah, showing that they lacked understanding.
