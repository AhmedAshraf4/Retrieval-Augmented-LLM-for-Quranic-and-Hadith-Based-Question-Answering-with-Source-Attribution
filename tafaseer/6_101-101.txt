Allah is the originator of the heavens and the earth, bringing them into existence without any prior example, as He alone created them from nothing. This is reflected in the term "Badi`," meaning something invented or brought into existence without precedence. Allah, being unique, does not have a child because a child requires two compatible spouses, and Allah has no equal or likeness. No being is comparable to Him, and He is the sole Creator of all things. How could He have a son, when He is beyond such attributes? Those who claim Allah has a child bring forth a grievous statement, as no creature resembles or is suitable to His majesty. He alone created everything and is fully aware of all matters.
