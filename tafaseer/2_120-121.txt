The Jews and Christians will never be pleased with Muhammad until he follows their religion, so he must not seek their approval but call to the guidance of Allah, which is the true, straight, and complete religion. If he were to follow their desires after receiving the Qur’an, he would have no protector or helper from Allah. Those who received the previous Scriptures, the Torah and the Gospel, recite them correctly by making lawful what they allow, forbidding what they prohibit, preserving the words as revealed, and interpreting them correctly. Those among them who fully adhere to their Scriptures will believe in the Qur’an and Muhammad’s prophethood, acknowledge the truth of what Allah sent, prostrate when it is recited, seek mercy or refuge according to its content, and act upon its commands. If they follow the Torah, the Gospel, and the Qur’an properly, they will be guided, provided for, and rewarded. Those who submit to Allah in Islam are rightly guided, while those who reject the Qur’an are the losers, and the Fire is promised for those who disbelieve. The Prophet affirmed that no one from his Ummah who hears of him and disbelieves, Jew or Christian, will escape this outcome.
