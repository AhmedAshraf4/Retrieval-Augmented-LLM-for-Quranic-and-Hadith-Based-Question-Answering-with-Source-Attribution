Adam and Hawwa began to cover themselves with leaves from Paradise after eating from the tree, which exposed their private parts. The leaves served as garments to conceal their nakedness. Previously, a light covering had hidden their private parts from each other. Upon realizing their exposure, Adam and Hawwa sought forgiveness from Allah, saying, "Our Lord! We have wronged ourselves. If You forgive us not, and bestow not upon us Your mercy, we shall certainly be of the losers." Allah accepted Adam's repentance, promising him entry into Paradise, while Shaytan, who sought only respite and not forgiveness, was granted his request.
