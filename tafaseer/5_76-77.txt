Allah rebukes those who worship idols or false deities, emphasizing that these objects have no power to harm or benefit anyone. He instructs Muhammad to ask the idolaters how they can worship what neither hears, sees, nor knows, while Allah is the All-Hearing, All-Knowing. Allah then addresses the People of the Scripture, warning them not to exceed the limits in their religion by falsely elevating `Isa, a Prophet, to the status of a god. This misbelief arose from following misguided leaders who led many astray from the right path.
