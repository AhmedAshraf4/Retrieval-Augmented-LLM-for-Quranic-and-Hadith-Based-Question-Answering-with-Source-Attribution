In times of distress or torment, idolaters call upon Allah alone, realizing that no one but Him can remove their affliction. When calamities strike, they forget the false deities they worshiped and turn only to Allah, as He is the sole power in those moments. Allah has tested previous nations with poverty and illness to encourage them to humble themselves and call upon Him in sincerity. However, when they ignored the warnings, Allah opened the gates of prosperity for them, leading them to rejoice in their wealth and blessings. But this enjoyment was a deception; suddenly, Allah's punishment overtook them, leaving them in deep regret and despair.
