Allah commands the believers to fully submit to Islam, following all its laws and commandments while avoiding its prohibitions. This includes abandoning any remaining adherence to previous scriptures like the Tawrah. The command to "enter Islam perfectly" was directed particularly at the People of the Scripture who had already believed in Allah but continued to follow parts of the older revelations. Allah warns them not to follow the footsteps of Shaytan, who leads to evil and false statements about Allah, urging them to reject his call, which aims to make his followers dwell in the fire. If they deviate after receiving clear signs, they should know that Allah is all-powerful in His punishment and wise in His decisions.
