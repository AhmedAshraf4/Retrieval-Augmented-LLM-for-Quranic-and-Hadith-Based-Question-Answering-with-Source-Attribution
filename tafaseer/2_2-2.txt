This is the Book in which there is no doubt, guidance for the God-conscious. The Book is the Qur’an, and it contains no doubt about its truth or its revelation from God. The correct reading treats “no doubt in it” as a complete statement, so guidance is a defining attribute of the Qur’an itself. The Qur’an is guidance in reality, but its benefit is realized only by those with taqwa. Guidance here includes the faith placed in the heart by God, which no human can create, and the clear explanation and direction that show the path of truth. The God-conscious are those who believe, avoid associating partners with God, obey Him, fear His punishment if they abandon known guidance, and hope for His mercy by believing in what He revealed. For them, the Qur’an is light, healing, and mercy, while those without faith do not benefit from it. Taqwa means guarding oneself from harm by careful restraint, like avoiding thorns while walking a dangerous path.
