Allah warns the believers against following the People of the Book, who, out of envy, wish to turn them back to disbelief after they have embraced faith. He reminds them that disbelief is inconceivable while the verses of Allah are being recited to them, and His Messenger is among them. Allah also questions how they could disbelieve when His Messenger calls them to believe, having taken their covenant. The Prophet highlighted that those who would come after the Companions, finding only scriptures, would have even more remarkable faith. Allah then emphasizes that whoever relies on Him is truly guided to the right path, as trust in Allah leads to guidance and away from the wrong path.
