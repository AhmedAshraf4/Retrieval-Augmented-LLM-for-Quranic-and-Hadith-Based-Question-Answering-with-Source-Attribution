Allah refutes those who prohibit certain foods, drinks, or clothes based on their own false opinions, without following what Allah has legislated. He commands Muhammad to ask the idolators who forbid such things, "Who has forbidden the adornment and provisions that Allah has created for His servants?" These things are meant for the believers who worship Allah in this life. Though disbelievers also benefit from these blessings in this world, the believers will possess them alone in the Hereafter, as Paradise is reserved exclusively for the believers and is forbidden to the disbelievers.
